gwyneth patricia dunwoody ( née phillips ; 12 december 1930 – 17 april 2008 ) was a british labour party politician , who was the member of parliament ( mp ) for exeter from 1966 to 1970 , and then for crewe ( later crewe and nantwich ) from february 1974 to her death in 2008 . she was a moderate socialism had a reputation as a fiercely independent parliamentarian , described as '' intelligent , obstinate , opinionated and hard-working '' .
