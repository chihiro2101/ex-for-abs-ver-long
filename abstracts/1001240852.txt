buzkashi , literally '' goat pulling '' in persian ) is a central asian sport in which horse-mounted players attempt to place a goat or calf carcass in a goal . similar games are known as kokpar , '' dom joly : know your kokpar from your kyz-kuu '' , the independent : columnists kupkari , '' traditions : kupkari '' , zoom central asia and ulak tartysh in kyrgyzstan and kazakhstan , and as kökbörü and gökbörü in turkey , where it is played mainly by communities originally from central asia . image : afghan game buzkashi.jpg thumb right 450px game of buzkashi in mazar-i-sharif , afghanistan
