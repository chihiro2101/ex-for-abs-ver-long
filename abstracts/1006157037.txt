bernard joseph butler ( born 1 may 1970 ) is an english musician , songwriter and record producer . he is best known as the first guitarist with suede , until his departure in 1994 . he has been hailed by some critics as the greatest guitarist of his generation ; '' bernard butler biography '' . allmusic bbc journalist mark savage called him '' one of britain 's most original and influential guitarists '' . '' the lowdown on brits bright stars '' . bbc news . 18 february 2009 he was voted the 24th greatest guitarist of the last 30 years in a national 2010 bbc poll '' the axe factor '' . bbc . retrieved 26 june 2010 . and is often seen performing with a 1961 cherry red gibson es-355 ( stereo varitone ) with a bigsby vibrato tailpiece .
