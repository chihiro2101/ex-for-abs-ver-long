disembowelment or evisceration is the removal of some or all of the organs of the gastrointestinal tract ( the bowels , or viscera ) , usually through a horizontal incision made across the abdomen area . disembowelment may result from an accident but has also been used as a method of torture and execution . in such practices , disembowelment may be accompanied by other forms of torture , or the removal of other vital organs .
