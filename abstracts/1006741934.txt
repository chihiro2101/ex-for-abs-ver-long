the last detail is a 1973 american comedy-drama film directed by hal ashby and starring jack nicholson , otis young , randy quaid , clifton james , michael moriarty and carol kane . the screenplay was written by robert towne , based on a 1969 novel of the same name by darryl ponicsan . it was released on december 12 , 1973 . the film became known for its frequent use of profanity , and was nominated for three academy awards : academy award for best actor ( nicholson ) , best actor in a supporting role ( quaid ) and academy award for best adapted screenplay .
