the dumbarton oaks conference or , more formally , the washington conversations on international peace and security organization was an international conference at which proposals for the establishment of a '' general international organization '' , which was to become the united nations , were formulated and negotiated . the conference was led by the big four – the united states , united kingdom , ussr and the republic of china – with delegates from other nations participating in the consideration and formulation of these principles . it was held at dumbarton oaks , in washington , d.c . , from august 21 , 1944 , to october 7 , 1944 .
