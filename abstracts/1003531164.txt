daniel drew ( july 29 , 1797 & ndash ; september 18 , 1879 ) was an american businessman , steamship and railroad developer , and financier . summarizing his life , henry clews wrote : '' of all the great operators of wall street . daniel drew furnishes the most remarkable instance of immense and long-continued success , followed by utter failure and hopeless bankruptcy '' . clews , henry . fifty years in wall street . hoboken , n.j . : j . wiley & sons , 2006 , p . 183 .
