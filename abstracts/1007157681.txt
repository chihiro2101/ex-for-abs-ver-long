the surfaris are an american surf rock band formed in glendora , california , united states , in 1962 . they are best known for two songs that hit the charts in the los angeles area , and nationally by may 1963 : '' surfer joe '' and '' wipe out '' , which were the a-side and b-side of the same 45 rpm single . the surfaris were inducted into the musicians hall of fame and museum in 2019 for their hit instrumental song , '' wipe out '' .
