general was a japanese imperial prince , a career officer in the imperial japanese army and the 30th prime minister of japan from 17 august 1945 to 9 october 1945 , a period of 54 days . an uncle-in-law of emperor hirohito twice over , prince higashikuni was the only member of the imperial house of japan to head a cabinet and was the last general officer of the imperial japanese military to become prime minister . he was the founder of the chiba institute of technology .
