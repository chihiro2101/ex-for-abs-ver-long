mca inc . ( originally an initialism for music corporation of america ) was an american media conglomerate founded in 1924 . originally a talent agency with artists in the music business as clients , the company became a major force in the film industry , and later expanded into television production . mca published music , booked acts , ran a record company , represented film , television , and radio stars , and eventually produced and sold television programs to the three major television networks , but had an especially good relationship with nbc . mca was the legal predecessor of vivendi and thereby nbcuniversal . its other legal successor is universal studios holding i corp , a holding company owned by vivendi , which also owns its former music assets through universal music group ( which has absorbed polygram ) and universal music publishing group .
