naseby is a village in the daventry ( district ) in northamptonshire , england . the population of the civil parish at the 2011 census was 687 . the village is 14 & nbsp ; mi ( 22.5 & nbsp ; km ) north of northampton , 13.3 & nbsp ; mi ( 21.4 & nbsp ; km ) northeast of daventry , and 7 & nbsp ; mi ( 11 & nbsp ; km ) south of market harborough .
