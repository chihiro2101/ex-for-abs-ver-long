number six is the central character in the 1960s television series the prisoner . the unnamed character in the original tv series was played by series co-creator patrick mcgoohan . for one episode , '' do not forsake me oh my darling '' , number six was portrayed by nigel stock due to mcgoohan being away filming the movie ice station zebra . in the amc remake , number six is played by jim caviezel ; in the big finish productions audio series of 2019 , number six is voiced by mark elstob .
