handcuffs are restraint devices designed to secure an individual 's wrists in proximity to each other . they comprise two parts , linked together by a chain , a hinge , or rigid bar . each cuff has a rotating arm which engages with a ratchet that prevents it from being opened once closed around a person 's wrist . without the key , the handcuffs can not be removed without specialist knowledge , and the handcuffed person can not move their wrists more than a few centimetres or inches apart , making many tasks difficult or impossible . handcuffs are frequently used by law enforcement agencies worldwide to prevent suspect from escaping from arrest .
