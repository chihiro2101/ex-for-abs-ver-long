the battle of white mountain ; was an important battle in the early stages of the thirty years ' war . it was fought on 8 november 1620 . an army of 15,000 bohemians and mercenaries under christian of anhalt was defeated by 27,000 men of the combined armies of ferdinand ii , holy roman emperor led by charles bonaventure de longueval , count of bucquoy and the german catholic league under johann tserclaes , count of tilly at bílá hora ( '' white mountain '' ) near prague . the battle of white mountain , 11-06-2003 - radio praguel . the site is now part of the city of prague .
