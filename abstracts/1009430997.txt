christie 's is a british auction house founded in 1766 by james christie . its main premises are on king street , st james 's in london and in rockefeller center in new york city . it is owned by groupe artémis , the holding company of françois-henri pinault . sales in 2015 totalled £4.8 billion ( us 7.4 billion ) . in 2017 , the salvator mundi was sold for 400 million at christie 's in new york , at the time the highest price ever paid for a single painting at an auction .
