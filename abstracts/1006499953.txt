worldwide pants inc . is an american television and film production company founded and owned by comedian and talk show host david letterman . the company was formerly headquartered at the ed sullivan theater building in new york city , but has since moved to los angeles following the ending of the late show with david letterman . the president and ceo is former late show executive producer rob burnett . peter lassally , a former the tonight show starring johnny carson and late show executive producer , was the senior vice-president until his retirement .
