the music of new zealand has been influenced by blues , jazz , country , rock and roll and hip hop , with many of these genres given a unique new zealand interpretation . a number of popular artists have gone on to achieve international success including lorde , split enz , crowded house , omc , bic runga , benee , kimbra , ladyhawke , the naked and famous , fat freddy 's drop , savage , gin wigmore , keith urban , flight of the conchords , and brooke fraser . pre-colonial māori music consisted mainly of a form of microtonal chanting and performances on instruments called taonga pūoro : a variety of blown , struck and twirled instruments made out of hollowed-out wood , stone , whale ivory , albatross bone , and human bone . in the nineteenth century , european settlers brought musical forms to new zealand including brass bands and choral music , and musicians began touring new zealand in the 1860s . pipe bands became widespread during the early 20th century . new zealand has a national orchestra , the new zealand symphony orchestra , and many regional orchestras . a number of new zealand composers have developed international reputations . the best-known include douglas lilburn , john psathas , jack body , gillian whitehead , jenny mcleod , gareth farr , and ross harris .
