tiye ( c . 1398 bc & ndash ; 1338 bc , also spelled tye , taia , tiy and tiyi ) was the daughter of yuya and tjuyu . she became the great royal wife of the egyptian pharaoh amenhotep iii . she was the mother of akhenaten and grandmother of tutankhamun . in 2010 , dna analysis confirmed her as the mummy known as '' the elder lady '' found in the tomb of amenhotep ii ( kv35 ) in 1898 .
