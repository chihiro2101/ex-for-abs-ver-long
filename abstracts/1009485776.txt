anton ivanovich denikin ( russian : анто́н ива́нович дени́кин , ipa : help : ipa/russian ɐnˈton ɪˈvanəvʲɪtɕ dʲɪˈnʲikʲɪn ; 16 december o.s . 4 december 1872 – 8 august 1947 ) was a russian lieutenant general in the imperial russian army ( 1916 ) , later served as the deputy supreme ruler of russia during the russian civil war of 1917–1922 . he was also a military leader of south russia ( as commander in chief ) .
