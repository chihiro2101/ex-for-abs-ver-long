marsden is a large village within the kirklees district , in west yorkshire , england . it is in the south pennines close to the peak district which lies to the south . the village is west of huddersfield at the confluence of the river colne and the wessenden brook . it was an important centre for the production of woollen cloth , focused at bank bottom mill , which closed in 2003 . according to a 2008 mid-year estimate the village has a population of 4,440 .
