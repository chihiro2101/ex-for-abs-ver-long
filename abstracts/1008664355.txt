halva ( also halvah , halwa , and other spellings ) refers to various local confection recipes in west asia and its vicinity . the name is used for referring to a huge variety of confections , with the most geographically common variety based on fried semolina . halva is also popular in central and south asia , the balkans , the caucasus , eastern europe , malta , north africa and the horn of africa . identical sweets exist in other countries , such as china , though these are not generally referred to as '' halva '' . halva can be kept at room temperature during non-summer months with little risk of spoilage .
