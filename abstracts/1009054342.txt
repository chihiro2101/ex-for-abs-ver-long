the concept of first world originated during the cold war and comprised countries that were aligned with nato and the united states , and opposed the soviet union and/or communism during the cold war . since the collapse of the soviet union in 1991 , the definition has instead largely shifted to any country with little political risk and a well functioning democracy , rule of law , capitalist economy , economic stability , and high standard of living . various ways in which modern first world countries are often determined include gdp , gnp , literacy rates , life expectancy , and the human development index . first world , investopedia in common usage , '' first world '' typically refers to '' the highly developed industrialized nations often considered the westernized countries of the world '' .
