civics is the study of the rights and obligations of citizens in society . civics at oxford dictionaries . the term derives from the latin word civicus , meaning '' relating to a citizen '' , which derives from the latin word civis , meaning '' citizen '' . the term relates to behavior affecting other citizens , particularly in the context of urban development . civic education is the study of the theoretical , political and practical aspects of citizenship , as well as its rights and duties . it includes the study of civil law and civil codes , and the study of government with attention to the role of citizens―as opposed to external factors―in the operation and oversight of government . the term can also refer to a civic crown , a garland of oak leaves worn about the head like a crown , a practice in ancient rome wherein someone who saved another roman citizen from death in war was rewarded with a corona civica and the right to wear it .
