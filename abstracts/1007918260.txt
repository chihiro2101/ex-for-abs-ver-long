assemblywomen ekklesiazousai ; also translated as , congresswomen , women in parliament , women in power , and a parliament of women ) is a comedy written by the greek playwright aristophanes in 391 bc . the play invents a scenario where the women of athens assume control of the government and instate reforms that ban private wealth and enforce sexual equity for the old and unattractive . in addition to aristophanes ' political and social satire , assemblywomen derives its comedy through sexual and scatological humor . it is important to note that the play aimed to criticize the athenian government at the time . zumbrunnen , john .
