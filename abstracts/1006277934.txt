elfquest ( or elfquest ) is a comic book property created by wendy and richard pini in 1978 . it is a fantasy story about a community of elves and other fictional species who struggle to survive and coexist on a primitive earth-like planet with two moons . several published volumes of prose fiction also share the same setting . elfquest was one of the first comic book series to have a planned conclusion . over the years elfquest has been self-published by the pinis through their own company warp graphics , then marvel comics , then the pinis again , more recently dc comics and then dark horse comics . all issues of elfquest published prior to 2014 are available online for free .
