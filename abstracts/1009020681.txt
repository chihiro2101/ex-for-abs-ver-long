the flag of north korea , also known as the ramhongsaek konghwagukgi ; literally '' blue and red-colored flag of the republic '' ) , consists of a central red panel , bordered both above and below by a narrow white stripe and a broad blue stripe . the central red panel bears a five-pointed red star within a white circle near the hoist . the flag is banned from public use in south korea due to its association with the ruling north korean regime , although some exceptions for the usage of the flag exist .
