sorry , wrong number is a 1948 american thriller film noir directed by anatole litvak and starring barbara stanwyck and burt lancaster . it tells the story of a woman who overhears a murder plot . the film was adapted by lucille fletcher from her 1943 radio play . stanwyck was nominated for the academy award for best actress . it is one of the few pre-1950 paramount pictures films that remained in the studio 's library ( the rest are currently owned by nbcuniversal ) .
