the sadducees ; ṣĕdûqîm ) were a sect or group of jews who were active in judea during the second temple period , starting from the second century bce through the destruction of the temple in 70 ce . the sadducees are often compared to other contemporaneous sects , including the pharisees and the essenes . josephus associates the sect with the upper social and economic echelon of judean society .
