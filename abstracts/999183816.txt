stardust is a 1999 fantasy novel by british writer neil gaiman , usually published with illustrations by charles vess . stardust has a different tone and style from most of gaiman 's prose fiction , being consciously written in the tradition of pre-tolkien english fantasy , following in the footsteps of authors such as lord dunsany and hope mirrlees . it is concerned with the adventures of a young man from the village of wall , which borders the magical land of faerie . in 2007 , a stardust ( 2007 film ) was released to generally positive reviews . gaiman has also occasionally made references to writing a sequel , or at least another book concerning the village of wall . 5 january 2007 , chat with barnes and noble , the story begins in late april 1839 , as john william draper had just photographed the moon and charles dickens was serialising oliver twist . the majority of the book takes place seventeen years later , starting around october 1856 .
