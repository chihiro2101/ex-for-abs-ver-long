love in a cold climate is a novel by nancy mitford , first published in 1949 . the title is a direct quotation from george orwell 's novel keep the aspidistra flying ( 1936 ) . love in a cold climate is a companion volume to the pursuit of love . the time frame of love in a cold climate is the same as the pursuit of love , but the focus is on a different set of characters . fanny remains the fictional narrator . in the pursuit of love , fanny narrates the story of her cousin linda radlett . in love in a cold climate , fanny narrates the story of polly , to whom fanny is distantly related through her father 's family .
