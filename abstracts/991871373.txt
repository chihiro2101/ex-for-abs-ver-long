image : frets , guitar neck , c-major chord.jpg thumb right the neck of a guitar showing the nut ( in the background , coloured white ) and first four metal frets a fret is a raised element on the neck of a stringed instrument . frets usually extend across the full width of the neck . on most modern western fretted instruments , frets are metal strips inserted into the fingerboard . on some historical instruments and non-european instruments , frets are made of pieces of string tied around the neck . frets divide the neck into fixed segments at intervals related to a musical framework . on instruments such as guitars , each fret represents one semitone in the standard western system , in which one octave is divided into twelve semitones . fret is often used as a verb , meaning simply '' to press down the string behind a fret '' . fretting often refers to the frets and/or their system of placement .
