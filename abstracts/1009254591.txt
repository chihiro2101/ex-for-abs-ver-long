oudenaarde ; ; in english sometimes oudenarde ) is a belgium municipality in the flemish province of east flanders . the municipality comprises the city of oudenaarde proper and the towns of bevere , edelare , eine , ename , heurne , leupegem , mater , melden , mullem , nederename , volkegem , welden and a part of ooike . from the 15th to the 18th century , but especially in the 16th century , oudenaarde was a world-known centre of tapestry production . the town 's name , meaning “ old field ” , still lingers on in “ outnal ” , an obsolete english term for a kind of brown linen thread .
