maurice gough gee ( born 22 august 1931 ) is a new zealand novelist . he is one of new zealand 's most distinguished and prolific authors , having written over thirty novels for adults and children , and has won numerous awards , including multiple top prizes at the new zealand book awards . his novel plumb ( 1978 ) is considered one of the best novels ever written in new zealand . in 1993 , andro linklater , writing in british newspaper the sunday times , said that '' gee deserves to be regarded as one of the finest writers at work , not only in new zealand . but in the english speaking world '' .
