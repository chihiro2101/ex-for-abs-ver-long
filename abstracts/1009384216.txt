w.a.s.p . is an american heavy metal band formed in 1982 . they emerged from the early 1980s los angeles heavy metal scene . the band 's popularity peaked that decade , yet they continue to record and tour , making them one of the most enduring of the west coast heavy metal bands . w.a.s.p . gained notoriety for their shock rock-themed image , lyrics and live performances . they have sold over 12 million albums . w.a.s.p . was a prominent target in the mid-1980s of the parents music resource center ( pmrc ) , an organization that pushed for warning labels on recorded music . the band immortalized its fight with the pmrc on the song '' harder , faster '' from their 1987 live album , live.in the raw . their most well-known songs include '' animal ( fuck like a beast ) '' , '' i wan na be somebody '' , '' l.o.v.e . machine '' , '' wild child '' , '' blind in texas '' , '' forever free '' , '' mean man '' , '' chainsaw charlie ( murders in the new morgue ) '' and '' the idol '' , as well as their cover versions of ray charles ' '' i do n't need no doctor '' and the who 's '' the real me '' . the band 's most recent album , golgotha , was released in 2015 , and they have reportedly been working on new material .
