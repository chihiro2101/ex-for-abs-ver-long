renée vivien ( 11 june 1877 – 18 november 1909 ) was a british poet who wrote in french , in the style of the symbolistes and the parnassiens . a high-profile lesbian in the paris of the belle époque , she is notable for her work , which has received more attention following a recent revival of interest in sappho . many of her poems are autobiographical , reflecting a life of extreme hedonism , leading to early death . she was the subject of a pen-portrait by her friend and neighbor colette .
