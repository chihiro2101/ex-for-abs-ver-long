london metropolitan university , commonly known as london met , is a public research university in london , england . the university of north london ( formerly the polytechnic of north london ) and london guildhall university ( formerly the city of london polytechnic ) merged in 2002 to create the university . the university 's roots go back to 1848 . the university has campuses in the city of london and in the london borough of islington , a museum , archives and libraries . special collections include the tuc library , the irish studies collection and the frederick parker collection . the frederick parker collection
