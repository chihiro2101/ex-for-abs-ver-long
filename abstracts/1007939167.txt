cumae or or ; perseus : κύ̂μα was the first ancient greek colony on the mainland of italy , founded by settlers from euboea in the 8th century bc and soon becoming one of the strongest colonies . it later became a rich roman city , the remains of which lie near the modern village of cuma , a frazione of the comune bacoli and pozzuoli in the metropolitan city of naples , campania , italy . the archaeological museum of the campi flegrei in the aragonese castle contains many finds from cumae .
