gopal krishna gokhale international phonetic alphabet ˈɡoːpaːl ˈkrɪʂɳə ˈɡoːkʰleː ( 9 may 1866 – 19 february 1915 ) was an indian liberal political leader and a social reformer during the indian independence movement . gokhale was a senior leader of the indian national congress and the founder of the servants of india society . through the society as well as the congress and other legislative bodies he served in , gokhale campaigned for indian self-rule and for social reforms . he was the leader of the moderate faction of the congress party that advocated reforms by working with existing government institutions .
