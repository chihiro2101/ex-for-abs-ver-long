the goldbergs is a comedy-drama broadcast from 1929 to 1946 on american radio , and from 1949 to 1956 on american television . it was adapted into a 1948 play , me and molly ; a 1950 film the goldbergs , and a 1973 broadway musical , molly . it also spun off briefly a comic strip from june 8 , 1944 to december 21 , 1945 with art by irving hasen , an artist at that time who worked in comic books especially on various titles published by dc comics . he would later do the dondi comic strip .
