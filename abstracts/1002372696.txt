james the great , also known as james , son of zebedee or as saint james the greater ( aramaic : ܝܥܩܘܒ ܒܪ ܙܒܕܝ ; arabic : يعقوب ; hebrew : , yaʿăqōḇ ; latin : iacobus maximus ; greek : ἰάκωβος ; died ad 44 ) , was one of the twelve apostles of jesus according to the new testament . saint james is the patron saint of spain and , according to tradition , his remains are held in santiago de compostela in galicia .
