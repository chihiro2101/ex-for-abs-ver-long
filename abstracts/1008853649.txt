a doctorate ( from latin docere , '' to teach '' ) or doctor 's degree ( from latin doctor , '' teacher '' ) or doctoral degree , is an academic degree awarded by universities , derived from the ancient formalism licentia docendi ( '' licence to teach '' ) . in most countries , it is a research degree that qualifies the holder to teach at university level in the degree 's field , or to work in a specific profession . there are a number of doctoral degrees ; the most common is the doctor of philosophy ( phd ) , which is awarded in many different fields , ranging from the humanities to science disciplines . in the united states and some other countries , there are also some types of technical or professional degrees that include doctor in their name and are , in some of those countries , classified as doctorates . professional doctorates historically came about to meet the needs of practitioners in a variety of disciplines . many universities also award honorary doctorates to individuals deemed worthy of special recognition , either for scholarly work or for other contributions to the university or to society .
