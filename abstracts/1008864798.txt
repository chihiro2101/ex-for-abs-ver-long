the cycle rickshaw is a small-scale local means of transport . it is a type of tricycle designed to carry passengers on a for-hire basis . it is also known by a variety of other names such as bike taxi , velotaxi , pedicab , bikecab , cyclo , beca , becak , trisikad , tricycle taxi , or trishaw . as opposed to rickshaws pulled by a person on foot , cycle rickshaws are human-powered by pedaling . another type of rickshaw is the auto rickshaw .
