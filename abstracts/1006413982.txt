luis vigoreaux rivera ( april 12 , 1928 – january 17 , 1983 ) was a puerto rican radio and television host , announcer , comedian and producer . vigoreaux was considered a pioneer in the television business in puerto rico , and enjoyed success with several radio and television shows throughout his career . vigoreaux was brutally murdered on january 17 , 1983 . his wife , lydia echevarría , was convicted in 1986 of planning the murder , along with hitmen papo newman and david lópez-watts .
