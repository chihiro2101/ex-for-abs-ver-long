vxworks is a real-time operating system ( rtos ) developed as proprietary software by wind river systems , a wholly owned subsidiary of tpg capital , us . first released in 1987 , vxworks is designed for use in embedded systems requiring real-time , deterministic performance and , in many cases , safety and security certification , for industries , such as aerospace and defense , medical devices , industrial equipment , robotics , energy , transportation , network infrastructure , automotive , and consumer electronics .
