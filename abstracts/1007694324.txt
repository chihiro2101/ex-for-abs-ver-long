the battle of varna took place on 10 november 1444 near varna in eastern bulgaria . the ottoman army under murad ii ( who did not actually rule the sultanate at the time ) defeated the hungary–poland and wallachian armies commanded by władysław iii of poland ( also king of hungary ) , john hunyadi ( acting as commander of the combined christian forces ) and mircea ii of wallachia . it was the final battle of the crusade of varna . bodnar , edward w . ciriaco d'ancona e la crociata di varna , nuove prospettive . il veltro 27 , nos . 1–2 ( 1983 ) : 235–51 halecki , oscar , the crusade of varna . new york , 1943
