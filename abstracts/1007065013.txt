roger i – 22 june 1101 ) , ( maltese : il-konti ruġġieru ) nicknamed roger bosso and the great count , was a norman nobleman who became the first count of sicily from 1071 to 1101 . he was a member of the house of hauteville , and his descendants in the male line continued to rule sicily down to 1194 . roger was born in normandy , and came to southern italy as a young man in 1057 . he participated in several military expeditions against the emirate of sicily beginning in 1061 . he was invested with part of sicily and the title of count by his brother , robert guiscard , duke of apulia , in 1071 . by 1090 , he had conquered the entire island . in 1091 , he conquered malta . the state he created was merged with the duchy of apulia in 1127 and became the kingdom of sicily in 1130 .
