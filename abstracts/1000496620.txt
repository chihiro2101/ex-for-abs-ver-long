the southern branches of osaka-ben , such as senshū-ben ( 泉州弁 ) and kawachi-ben ( 河内弁 ) , are famous for their harsh locution , characterized by trilled '' r '' , the question particle ke , and the second person ware . the farther south in osaka one goes , the cruder the language is considered to be , with the local senshū-ben of kishiwada said to represent the peak of harshness . riichi nakaba ( 2005 ) . kishiwada shonen gurentai . kodansha .
