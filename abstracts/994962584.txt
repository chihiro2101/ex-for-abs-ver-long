the gens petronia was a plebeian family at ancient rome . this gens claimed an ancient lineage , as a petronius sabinus is mentioned in the time of lucius tarquinius superbus , the last of the roman kings , but few petronii are mentioned in the time of the republic . they are frequently encountered under the empire , holding numerous consulships , and eventually obtaining the empire itself during the brief reign of petronius maximus in ad 455 . dictionary of greek and roman biography and mythology , vol . iii , p . 215 ( '' petronia gens '' ) .
