thumb a pub quiz is a quiz held in a pub or bar . these events are also called quiz nights , trivia nights , or bar trivia and may be held in other settings . pub quizzes may attract customers to a pub who are not found there on other days . the pub quiz is a modern example of a pub game . although different pub quizzes can cover a range of formats and topics , they have many features in common . the pub quiz was established in the uk in the 1970s by burns and porter and became part of british culture . the great british pub quiz challenge is an annual event . pub quizzes are a staple event at irish pubs , where they are usually held in english .
