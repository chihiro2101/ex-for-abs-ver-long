felt is a textile material that is produced by matting , condensing and pressing fibers together . felt can be made of natural fibers such as wool or animal fur , or from synthetic fibers such as petroleum-based acrylic or acrylonitrile or wood pulp-based rayon . blended fibers are also common . felt has special properties that allow it to be used for a wide variety of purposes . '' it is fire-retardant and self-extinguishing ; it dampens vibration and absorbs sound ; and it can hold large amounts of fluid without feeling wet . ''
