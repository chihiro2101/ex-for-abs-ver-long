zefram cochrane is a character ( arts ) in the star trek universe . created by writer gene l . coon , the character first appeared in the 1967 star trek episode '' metamorphosis '' , in which he was played by glenn corbett . james cromwell later played cochrane in the 1996 feature film star trek : first contact and the 2001 star trek : enterprise pilot , '' broken bow '' . footage of cromwell from star trek : first contact was used in the enterprise episode '' in a mirror , darkly '' . as first mentioned in star trek : the original series , and further established by the events of star trek : first contact , cochrane is the first human to create a warp drive system , and in 2063 , his successful warp speed flight draws the attention of the vulcans , leading to humanity 's first official contact with an alien race .
