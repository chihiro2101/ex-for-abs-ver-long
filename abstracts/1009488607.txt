swithun ( or swithin , , ; died 863 ad ) was an anglo-saxon bishop of winchester and subsequently patron saint of winchester cathedral . his historical importance as bishop is overshadowed by his reputation for posthumous miracle-working . according to tradition , if it rains on saint swithun 's bridge ( winchester ) on his feast day ( 15 july ) it will continue for forty days . the name was originally spelt swithhun ( old english : '' strong bear-cub '' ) . , footnote .
