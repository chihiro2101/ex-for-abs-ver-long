mouscron ; , ; west flemish and picard : moucron ) is a walloon city and walloons municipality with facilities for the dutchspeaking minority , located in the belgium province of hainaut , along the border with the french city of tourcoing , which is part of the lille metropolitan area . the mouscron municipality also includes the old municipalities of dottignies , luingne , and herseaux . in accordance with the national law , the municipality offers dual language services in french and dutch . kortrijk , in flanders , is located just to the north of mouscron . the city substantially grew during the 19th and early 20th century with the development of the textile industry in the north of france . the symbol of the city is the hurlu : a character representing geuzen , who plundered the countryside during the wars of religion ( 16th century ) .
