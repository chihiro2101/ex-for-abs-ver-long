elizabeth palmer peabody ( may 16 , 1804january 3 , 1894 ) was an american educator who opened the first english-language kindergarten in the united states . long before most educators , peabody embraced the premise that children 's play has intrinsic developmental and educational value . peabody was also the first translator into english of the buddhism scripture the lotus sutra , translating a chapter from its french translation in 1844 .
