corfe castle is a village and civil parish in the english county of dorset . it is the site of a ruined castle of the same name . the village and castle stand over a gap in the purbeck hills on the route between wareham and swanage . the village lies in the gap below the castle and is around south-east of wareham , and north-northwest of swanage . both the main a351 road from lytchett minster to swanage and the swanage railway thread their way through the gap and the village . the civil parish of corfe castle stretches across the width of the isle of purbeck , with coasts facing both the english channel and poole harbour . it , therefore , includes sections of both the low-lying sandy heathland that lies to the north of the castle and the rugged jurassic coast upland to the south .
