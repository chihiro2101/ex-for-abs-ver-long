miguel lópez de legazpi ; c . 1502 & nbsp ; – august 20 , 1572 ) , also known as adelantado and el viejo ( the elder ) , was a spanish navigator and governor who established the first spanish settlement in the east indies when his expedition crossed the pacific ocean from the new spain in modern-day mexico , arriving in cebu in the philippines in 1565 . he was the first governor-general of the spanish east indies , which was governed and mainly located in the philippines . it also encompassed other pacific islands namely guam and the mariana islands . after obtaining peace with various indigenous nations and kingdoms , he made cebu city the capital of the spanish east indies in 1565 and later transferred to manila in 1571 . the capital city of the province of albay bears his name .
