the magellanic clouds ( or nubeculae magellani ) are two irregular dwarf galaxies visible in the southern celestial hemisphere ; they are members of the local group and are orbiting the milky way galaxy . because both show signs of a bar structure , they are often reclassified as magellanic spiral galaxies . the two galaxies are : large magellanic cloud ( lmc ) , approximately 163,000 light-years away small magellanic cloud ( smc ) , approximately 206,000 light years away
