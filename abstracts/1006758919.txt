the battle of killiecrankie , also referred to as the battle of rinrory , took place on 27 july 1689 during the jacobite rising of 1689 . a jacobite force under john graham , viscount dundee defeated a government army commanded by hugh mackay . james ii & vii went into exile in december 1688 after being deposed by the glorious revolution in scotland . in march 1689 , he began the williamite war in ireland , with a simultaneous revolt led by dundee , previously military commander in scotland . hampered by lack of men and resources , dundee gambled on a decisive battle which he hoped would attract wider support . although killiecrankie was an unexpected and stunning victory , his army suffered heavy casualties and he was killed in the final minutes . it did little to change the overall strategic position , and the jacobites were unable to take advantage of their success .
