mottram in longdendale is an unparished village within the tameside , in greater manchester , england . the 2011 census for the ward of longdendale , which includes mottram and the surrounding area , was 9,950 . historically part of cheshire , it lies in the valley of longdendale , on the border with derbyshire and close to the peak district neighbouring broadbottom and hattersley . mottram in longdendale parish was one of the eight ancient parishes of the macclesfield hundred of cheshire . the larger mottram parish was incorporated into longdendale in 1936 , remaining part of cheshire , then incorporated into tameside as part of the provisions of the local government act 1972 in 1974 . even as late as 1991 , the town has the preferred name of mottram in longdendale .
