sir john francis edward acton , 6th baronet ( 3 june 1736 12 august 1811 ) was a french-born english gentleman who served as commander of the naval forces of the grand duchy of tuscany and later as prime minister of the kingdom of naples under ferdinand iv . chambers biographical dictionary , , p.6 he served at naples during the turbulent times when french revolutionary fervour under napoleon was sweeping across europe and threatening to extinguish the monarchy he served .
