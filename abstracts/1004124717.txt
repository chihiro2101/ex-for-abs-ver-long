caerleon ; is a town and community on the river usk situated northeast of the city of newport , wales and southeast of cwmbran . caerleon is of archaeological importance , being the site of a notable roman legionary castra , isca augusta , and an iron age hillfort . close to the remains of isca augusta are the national roman legion museum and the roman baths museum . the town also has strong historical and literary associations : geoffrey of monmouth elevated the significance of caerleon as a major centre of british history in his historia regum britanniae ( c.1136 ) , and alfred lord tennyson wrote idylls of the king ( 1859-1885 ) while staying in caerleon .
