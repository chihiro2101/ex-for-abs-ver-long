giorgos or george seferis ; , the pen name of georgios seferiades ( γεώργιος σεφεριάδης ; – september 20 , 1971 ) , was a greek poet-diplomat . he was one of the most important greek poets of the 20th century , and a nobel laureate . he was a career diplomat in the greek foreign service , culminating in his appointment as ambassador to the uk , a post which he held from 1957 to 1962 .
