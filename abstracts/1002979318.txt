big rock candy mountain '' , first recorded by harry mcclintock in 1928 , is a country folk song about a hobo 's idea of paradise , a modern version of the medieval concept of cockaigne . it is a place where '' hens lay soft-boiled egg '' and there are '' cigarette trees . '' mcclintock claimed to have written the song in 1895 , based on tales from his youth hoboing through the united states , but some believe that at least aspects of the song have existed for far longer . it is catalogued as roud folk song index no . 6696 .
