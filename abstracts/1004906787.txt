paul johann ludwig von heyse ; 15 march 1830 – 2 april 1914 ) was a distinguished german writer and translator . a member of two important literary societies , the tunnel über der spree in berlin and die krokodile in munich , he wrote novels , poetry , 177 short stories , and about sixty dramas . the sum of heyse 's many and varied productions made him a dominant figure among german men of letters . he was awarded the nobel prize for literature in 1910 '' as a tribute to the consummate artistry , permeated with idealism , which he has demonstrated during his long productive career as a lyric poet , dramatist , novelist and writer of world-renowned short stories . '' wirsen , one of the nobel judges , said that '' germany has not had a greater literary genius since goethe . '' heyse is the fifth oldest laureate in literature , after alice munro , jaroslav seifert , theodor mommsen and doris lessing .
