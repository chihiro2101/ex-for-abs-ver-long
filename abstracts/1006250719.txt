the pearl is a novella by the american author john steinbeck . the story , first published in 1947 , follows a pearl diver , kino , and explores man 's nature as well as greed , defiance of societal norms , and evil . steinbeck 's inspiration was a mexican folk tale from la paz , baja california sur , mexico , which he had heard in a visit to the formerly pearl-rich region in 1940 . the book was adapted as a mexican film named la perla ( 1947 ) and as a cult kannada movie ondu muttina kathe ( 1987 ) . the story is one of steinbeck 's most popular books and has been widely used in high school classes . the pearl is sometimes considered a parable .
