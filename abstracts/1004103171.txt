vincent clair gair ( 25 february 190111 november 1980 ) was an australian politician . he served as premier of queensland from 1952 until 1957 , when his stormy relations with the trade union movement saw him expelled from the labor party . he was elected to the australian senate and led the democratic labor party from 1965 to 1973 . in 1974 he was appointed australian ambassador to ireland by the whitlam government , which caused his expulsion from the dlp .
