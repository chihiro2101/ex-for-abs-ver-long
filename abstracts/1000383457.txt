ermanno wolf-ferrari ( born ermanno wolf ) ( january 12 , 1876 & ndash ; january 21 , 1948 ) was an italy composer and teacher . he is best known for his comic operas such as il segreto di susanna ( 1909 ) . a number of his works were based on plays by carlo goldoni , including le donne curiose ( 1903 ) , i quatro rusteghi ( 1906 ) and il campiello ( 1936 ) .
