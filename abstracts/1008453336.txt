a culture war is a cultural conflict between social groups and the struggle for dominance of their values , beliefs , and practices . it commonly refers to topics on which there is general societal disagreement and polarization in societal values is seen . the term is commonly used to describe contemporary politics in the united states , with issues such as abortion , homosexuality , transgender rights , pornography , multiculturalism , racial viewpoints and other cultural conflicts based on values , morality , and lifestyle which are described as the major political cleavage .
