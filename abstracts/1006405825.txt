an almanac ( also spelled almanack and almanach ) is an annual publication listing a set of events forthcoming in the next year . it includes information like weather forecasts , farmers ' sowing dates , tide tables , and other tabular data often arranged according to the calendar . celestial figures and various statistics are found in almanacs , such as the rising and sunset times of the sun and moon , dates of eclipses , hours of high and low tides , and religious festivals . the set of events noted in an almanac may be tailored for a specific group of readers , such as farmers , sailors , or astronomers .
