girolamo fracastoro ; c . 1476/86 august 1553 the date given in the vita is '' octavo idus augusti mdliii . '' ( in : opera omnia , 1555 ; 1574 ; 1584 ) ; for conversion see e.g . here . ) was an italian physician , poet , and scholar in mathematics , geography and astronomy . fracastoro subscribed to the philosophy of atomism , and rejected appeals to hidden causes in scientific investigation . his studies of the mode of syphilis transmission are an early example of epidemiology .
