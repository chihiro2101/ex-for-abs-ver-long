the term happiness is used in the context of mental or emotional states , including positive or pleasure emotions ranging from contentment to intense joy . it is also used in the context of life satisfaction , subjective well-being , eudaimonia , flourishing and well-being . see definition section below . since the 1960s , happiness research has been conducted in a wide variety of scientific disciplines , including gerontology , social psychology and positive psychology , clinical and medical research and happiness economics .
