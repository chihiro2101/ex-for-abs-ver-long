walla walla university is a private adventist university in college place , washington . the university has five campuses throughout the pacific northwest . it was founded in 1892 and is affiliated with the seventh-day adventist church . the university has an annual enrollment of around 1,900 students . it is regionally accredited by the northwest association of schools and colleges and is also denominationally accredited . walla walla university offers more than 100 areas of study including preprofessional degrees and four graduate programs . the edward f . cross school of engineering is an abet accredited program that offers a bachelor of science and engineering degrees . students are organized as the associated students of walla walla university or aswwu . aswwu operates the student newspaper the collegian , the annual mountain ash yearbook ( a collection of photographs depicting events of the school year and student portraits ) , and the online student directory found at aswwu.com . both the men 's and the women 's on-campus residence halls operate their own clubs . the women 's club is named aleph gimel ain ( aga ) ; the men 's club is named omicron pi sigma ( ops ) . in athletics , walla walla university competes as a member of the naia association of independent institutions ( aii ) . their team name is '' the wolves '' .
