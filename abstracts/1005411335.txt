fairchild semiconductor international , inc . was an american semiconductor company based in san jose , california . founded in 1957 as a division of fairchild camera and instrument , it became a pioneer in the manufacturing of transistors and of integrated circuits . schlumberger bought the firm in 1979 and sold it to national semiconductor in 1987 ; fairchild was spun off as an independent company again in 1997 . in september 2016 , fairchild was acquired by on semiconductor . the company had locations in the united states at san jose , california ; san rafael , california ; south portland , maine ; west jordan , utah ; and mountaintop , pennsylvania . outside the u.s . it operated locations in singapore ; bucheon ; penang , malaysia ; suzhou ; and cebu city , among others .
