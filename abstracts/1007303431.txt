molly malone '' ( also known as '' cockles and mussels '' or '' in dublin 's fair city '' ) is a popular song set in dublin , ireland , which has become its unofficial anthem . a statue representing molly malone was unveiled on grafton street by then lord mayor of dublin , ben briscoe , during the 1988 dublin millennium celebrations , when 13 june was declared to be molly malone day . in july 2014 , the statue was relocated to suffolk street , in front of the tourist information office , to make way for luas track-laying work at the old location .
