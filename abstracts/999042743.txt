the ford taunus is a family car that was sold by ford germany throughout europe . models from 1970 onward were built on the same basic construction as the ford cortina mkiii in the united kingdom , and later on , the two car models were rebadging , differing almost only in the placement of the steering wheel . the model line was named after the taunus mountain range in germany , and was first made in 1939 , and continued through several versions until 1994 .
