puff , the magic dragon '' ( or just '' puff '' ) is a song written by peter yarrow of peter , paul and mary from a poem by lenny lipton . it was made popular by yarrow 's group in a 1962 recording released in january 1963 . lipton wrote a poem about a dragon in 1959 , and when yarrow found it , he wrote the lyrics to '' puff '' based on the poem . after the song was released , yarrow searched for lipton to give him credit for the song .
