thumb racetrack is a paper and pencil game that simulates a car race , played by two or more players . the game is played on a squared sheet of paper , with a pencil line tracking each car 's movement . the rules for moving represent a car with a certain inertia and physical limits on traction , and the resulting line is reminiscent of how real racing cars move . the game requires players to slow down before bends in the track , and requires some foresight and planning for successful play . the game is popular as an educational tool teaching vectors . the game is also known under names such as vector formula , vector rally , vector race , graph racers , polyrace , paper and pencil racing , or the graph paper race game .
