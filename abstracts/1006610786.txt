billy liar is a 1959 novel by keith waterhouse that was later adapted into a play , a film , a musical and a tv series . the work has inspired and been featured in a number of popular songs . the semi-comical story is about william fisher , a working-class 19-year-old living with his parents in the fictional town of stradhoughton in yorkshire . bored by his job as a lowly clerk for an undertaker , billy spends his time indulging in fantasies and dreams of life in the big city as a comedy writer .
