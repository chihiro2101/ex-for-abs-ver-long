sejong the great ; 15 may 1397 – 8 april 1450 ) was the fourth king of the joseon dynasty of korea . he was the third son of king taejong and queen consort min . sejong was designated as crown prince after his older brother prince yangnyeong was stripped of his title . he ascended to the throne in 1418 . during the first four years of sejong 's reign , taejong governed as regent and executed sejong 's father-in-law , sim on , and his close associates . sejong reinforced korean confucian and neo-confucian policies , and enacted major legal amendments ; . he personally created and promulgated the hangul , encouraged advancements of science and technology , and introduced measures to stimulate economic growth . he dispatched military campaigns to the north and instituted the policy ; to attract new settlers to the region . to the south , he helped subjugate wokou , and in the ōei invasion capture tsushima island ( also known as daema island in the korean language ) . during his reign from 1418 to 1450 , sejong governed along with taejong from 1418 to 1422 . he then governed as the sole monarch from 1422 to 1450 , although after 1439 he became increasingly ill , and beginning in 1442 , his son , crown prince munjong , acted as regent for him .
