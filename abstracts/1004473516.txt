the skagerrak , also , '' skagerrak '' ( us ) and , , is a strait running between the southeast coast of norway , the west coast of sweden , and the jutland of denmark , connecting the north sea and the kattegat sea area , which leads to the baltic sea . the skagerrak contains some of the busiest shipping routes in the world , with vessels from every corner of the globe . it also supports an intensive fishing industry . the ecosystem is strained and negatively affected by direct human activities . oslo and gothenburg are the only large cities in the skagerrak region .
