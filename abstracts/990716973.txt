robert duane ballard ( born june 30 , 1942 ) is a retired american navy officer and a professor of oceanography at the university of rhode island who is most noted for his work in underwater archaeology : maritime archaeology and archaeology of shipwrecks . he is most known for the discoveries of the wrecks of the rms titanic in 1985 , the battleship bismarck in 1989 , and the aircraft carrier in 1998 . he discovered the wreck of john f . kennedy 's pt-109 in 2002 and visited biuku gasa and eroni kumana , who saved its crew . he leads ocean exploration on ev nautilus .
