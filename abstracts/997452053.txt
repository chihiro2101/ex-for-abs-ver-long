the spam prevention early warning system ( spews ) was an anonymous service which maintained a list of ip address ranges belonging to internet service providers ( isps ) which host spammers and show little action to prevent their abuse of other networks ' resources . it could be used by internet sites as an additional source of information about the senders of unsolicited bulk email , better known as spam . spews is no longer active . a successor , the anonymous postmaster early warning system ( apews ) , appeared in january 2007 . apews announcement of spews replacement
