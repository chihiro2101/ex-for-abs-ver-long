the lavender hill mob is a 1951 comedy film from ealing studios , written by t . e . b . clarke , directed by charles crichton , starring alec guinness and stanley holloway and featuring sid james and alfie bass . the title refers to lavender hill , a street in battersea , a district of south london , in the postcode district sw11 , near to clapham junction railway station . the british film institute ranked the lavender hill mob the bfi top 100 british films . the original film was digitally restored and re-released to uk cinemas on 29 july 2011 to celebrate its 60th anniversary . the guardian 15 july 2011 : cream of the cockney crop retrieved 19 april 2012
