the m1 motorway connects london to leeds , where it joins the a1 ( m ) near aberford , to connect to newcastle . it was the first inter-urban motorway to be completed in the uk ; the first motorway in the country was the preston by-pass , which later became part of the m6 . the motorway is long and was constructed in four phases . most of the motorway was opened between 1959 and 1968 . the southern end was extended in 1977 and the northern end was extended in 1999 . it forms part of the unsigned european route e13 .
