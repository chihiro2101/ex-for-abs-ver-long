shoreham-by-sea ( often shortened to shoreham ) is a coastal town and port in west sussex , england . the town is bordered to its north by the south downs , to its west by the adur valley and to its south by the river adur and shoreham beach on the english channel . the town lies in the middle of the ribbon of urban development along the english south coast , approximately equidistant from the city of brighton and hove to the east and the town of worthing to the west . shoreham covers an area of and has a population of 20,547 ( 2011 census ) .
