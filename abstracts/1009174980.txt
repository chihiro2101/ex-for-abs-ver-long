the persimmon is the edible fruit of a number of species of trees in the genus diospyros . the most widely cultivated of these is the oriental persimmon , diospyros kaki . diospyros is in the family ebenaceae , and a number of non-persimmon species of the genus are grown for ebony timber . in 2019 , china produced 75 % of the world total of persimmons . thumb
