aldrich hazen '' rick '' ames ; born may 26 , 1941 ) is a former central intelligence agency ( cia ) officer turned kgb double agent , who was convicted of espionage in 1994 . he is serving a life sentence , without the possibility of parole , in the federal correctional institution in terre haute , indiana , united states .
