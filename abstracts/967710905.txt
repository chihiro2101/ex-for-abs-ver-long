smpte timecode or is a set of cooperating standards to label individual frames of video or film with a timecode . the system is defined by the society of motion picture and television engineers in the smpte 12m specification . smpte revised the standard in 2008 , turning it into a two-part document : smpte 12m-1 and smpte 12m-2 , including new explanations and clarifications . timecodes are added to film , video or audio material , and have also been adapted to synchronize music and theatrical production . they provide a time reference for editing , synchronization and identification . timecode is a form of media metadata . the invention of timecode made modern videotape editing possible and led eventually to the creation of non-linear editing systems .
