1776 is a musical with music and lyrics by sherman edwards and a book by peter stone . the show is based on the events leading up to the signing of the declaration of independence , telling a story of the efforts of john adams to persuade his colleagues to vote for american independence and to sign the document . the show premiered on broadway in 1969 , earning warm reviews , and ran for 1,217 performances . the production won three tony awards , including best musical . in 1972 , it was made into 1776 ( film ) . it was revived on broadway in 1997 ; another broadway revival is scheduled for 2021 .
