i remember mama is a 1948 american drama film directed by george stevens from a screenplay by dewitt bodeen , whose work was adapted from john van druten 's stage play . druten , in turn , had based his play on kathryn forbes ' novel mama 's bank account , which was originally published by harcourt brace in 1943 . the story in all its variant forms recounts the everyday life and economic struggles of a norwegian immigrant family in san francisco in the early 20th century . the film stars irene dunne in the titular role , as well as barbara bel geddes , oscar homolka , ellen corby and philip dorn . homolka portrays uncle chris in the film , a role he had performed earlier in the broadway production . the film was nominated for five academy awards . dirks , tim .
