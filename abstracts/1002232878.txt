kirkburton is a village , civil parish and local government ward in the metropolitan county of west yorkshire , england , south east of huddersfield , in the kirklees . historically part of the west riding of yorkshire , the township comprises the villages of kirkburton and highburton and several hamlets , including thunder bridge , thorncliff , storthes hall , burton royd , riley , dogley , common side , causeway foot , lane head and linfit . according to the 2011 census the civil parish had a population of 26,439 , while the village itself had a population of 4,299 . : '' census 2011 : kirklees localities '' retrieved 2 june 2014
