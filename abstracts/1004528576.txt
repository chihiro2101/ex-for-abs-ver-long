donald serrell thomas ( born 18 july 1934 the balliol college register . fifth edition , 1930-1980 . edited by john jones and sally viney ( 1983 ) , p . 294 . ) is an english author of ( primarily ) victorian era historical , crime and detective novels , as well as books on factual crime and criminals , in particular several academic books on the history of crime in london . he has written a number of biographies , two volumes of poetry , and has also edited volumes of poetry by john dryden and the pre-raphaelites .
