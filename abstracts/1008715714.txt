the lentil ( lens culinaris or lens esculenta ) is an edible legume . it is an annual plant known for its lens-shaped seeds . it is about tall , and the seeds grow in pods , usually with two seeds in each . as a food crop , the majority of world production comes from canada and india , producing 58 % combined of the world total . in cuisines of the indian subcontinent , where lentils are a staple , split lentils ( often with their hulls removed ) known as dal are often cooked into a thick curry/gravy that is usually eaten with rice or rotis .
