tourism in russia has seen rapid growth since the late soviet times , first domestic tourism and then international tourism as well . rich cultural heritage and great natural variety place russia among the world tourism rankings in the world . not including crimea , the country list of world heritage sites in russia , while many more are on unesco 's tentative lists . major tourist routes in russia include a travel around the golden ring of ancient cities , cruises on the big rivers including the volga , and long journeys on the famous trans-siberian railway . diverse regions and ethnic cultures of russia offer many different foods and souvenirs , and show a great variety of traditions , including russian maslenitsa , tatar sabantuy , or siberian shamanist rituals . in 2013 , russia was visited by 33 million tourists , making it the ninth-most visited country in the world and the seventh-most visited in europe .
