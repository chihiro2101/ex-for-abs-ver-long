image : sommarnöje ( 1886 ) , akvarell av anders zorn.jpg thumb sommarnöje , 1886 . sweden 's priciest painting ever ; sold at 26 & nbsp ; million sek on june 3 , 2010 . anders leonard zorn ( 18 february 1860 – 22 august 1920 ) was one of sweden 's foremost artists . he obtained international success as a painter , sculptor , and etcher . among zorn 's portrait subjects were king oscar ii of sweden , and three american presidents , grover cleveland , william h . taft , and theodore roosevelt . at the end of his life , he established the swedish literary bellman prize in 1920 .
