arthur is a 1981 american comedy film written and directed by steve gordon . it stars dudley moore as arthur bach , a drunken new york city billionaire who is on the brink of an arranged marriage to a wealthy heiress , but ends up falling for a common working-class girl from queens . it was the sole film directed by gordon , who died in 1982 of a heart attack at age 44 . the film earned over 95 million domestically , making it the 1981 in film . its title song , '' arthur 's theme ( best that you can do ) '' , won the academy award for best original song . co-written by christopher cross , burt bacharach , carole bayer sager and peter allen , it was performed by christopher cross . john gielgud also won an oscar for best supporting actor . it was nominated for two other academy awards .
