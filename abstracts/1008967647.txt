mclaughlin motor car company limited was a canada manufacturer of automobiles headquartered in oshawa , ontario . the company , founded by robert mclaughlin , was originally the largest carriage manufacturing company in the british empire . by 1907 it had grown to include the manufacture of mclaughlin automobiles with buick engines and in 1915 , it manufactured chevrolet vehicles for the canadian market . the carriage end of the business was then sold to the carriage factories ltd . of orillia , ontario . mclaughlin was acquired by general motors in 1918 and it became general motors canada , a subsidiary of the us-based company .
