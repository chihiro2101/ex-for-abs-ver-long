lana lang is a fictional supporting character appearing in american comic books published by dc comics . she has appeared in other media adaptations of superman , typically as a teenager . these portrayals include the superboy ( tv series ) television series in which stacy haiduk played her , and the wb/cw television series smallville played by kristin kreuk . in superman : the movie , lana was played by diane sherry . in the 1983 film superman iii , she was played by annette o'toole , who would later portray martha kent on smallville . emmanuelle chriqui portrays the character in the arrowverse television series superman & lois .
