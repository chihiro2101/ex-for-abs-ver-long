the concert for george was held at the royal albert hall in london on 29 november 2002 as a memorial to george harrison on the first anniversary of his death . the concert for george , catalogue number : 0349702412 the event was organised by harrison 's widow , olivia , and his son , dhani , and arranged under the musical direction of eric clapton . the profits from the event went to the material world charitable foundation , an organisation founded by harrison .
