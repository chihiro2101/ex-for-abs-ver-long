davidson black , frs ( july 25 , 1884 & ndash ; march 15 , 1934 ) was a canadian paleoanthropologist , best known for his naming of sinanthropus pekinensis ( now homo erectus pekinensis ) . he was chairman of the geological survey of china and a fellow of the royal society . he was known as 步達生 ( pinyin : bù dáshēng ) in china .
