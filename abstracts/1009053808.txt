pontefract is a historic market town in the metropolitan borough of wakefield in west yorkshire , england , east of wakefield and south of castleford . historically part of the west riding of yorkshire , it is one of the towns in the city of wakefield district and had a population of 30,881 at the 2011 census . pontefract 's motto is , latin for '' after the death of the father , support the son '' , a reference to the town 's royalist sympathies in the english civil war . padgett 170
