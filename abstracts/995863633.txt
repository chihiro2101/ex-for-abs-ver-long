the american psychiatric association ( apa ) is the main professional organization of psychiatrists and trainee psychiatrists in the united states , and the largest psychiatry organization in the world . its some 38,800 members are mainly american but some are international . the association publishes various journals and pamphlets , as well as the diagnostic and statistical manual of mental disorders ( dsm ) . the dsm codifies psychiatric conditions and is used worldwide as a guide for diagnosing disorders . the organization has its headquarters in washington , d.c .
