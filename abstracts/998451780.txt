image : stampederodeo2002.jpg thumb right upright 1.5 bareback bronc riding at a rodeo . bronc riding , either bareback bronc or saddle bronc competition , is a rodeo event that involves a rodeo participant riding a bucking horse ( sometimes called a bronc or bronco ) that attempts to throw or bucking the rider . originally based on the necessary horse breaking skills of a working cowboy , the event is now a highly stylized competition that utilizes horses that often are specially bred for strength , agility , and bucking ability . it is recognized by the professional rodeo cowboys association ( prca ) and the international professional rodeo association ( ipra ) .
