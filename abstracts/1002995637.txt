telecine or is the process of transferring film film into video and is performed in a color suite . the term is also used to refer to the equipment used in the post-production process . telecine enables a motion picture , captured originally on film stock , to be viewed with standard video equipment , such as television sets , video cassette recorders ( vcr ) , dvd , blu-ray disc or computers . initially , this allowed television broadcasters to produce programmes using film , usually 16 mm film , but transmit them in the same format , and quality , as other forms of television production . furthermore , telecine allows film producers , television producers and film distributors working in the film industry to release their products on video and allows producers to use video production equipment to complete their filmmaking projects . within the film industry , it is also referred to as a tk , because tc is already used to designate timecode . motion picture film scanners are similar to telecines .
