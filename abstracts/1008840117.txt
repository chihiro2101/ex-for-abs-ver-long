islamic fundamentalism has been defined as a movement of muslims who are of the view that muslim majority countries should return to the fundamentals of an islamic state , which truly show the essence of the system of islam , in terms of its socio-politico-economic system . islamic fundamentalists favor '' a literal and originalist interpretation '' of the primary sources of islam ( the quran and sunnah ) , seek to eliminate ( what they perceive to be ) '' corrupting '' non-islamic influences from every part of their lives orfpi1994 : p . 215 and see '' islamic fundamentalism '' as a pejorative term used by outsiders for islamic revivalism and islamic activism .
