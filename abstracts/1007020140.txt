a castell is a human tower built traditionally at festivals in catalonia , the balearic islands and the valencian community . at these festivals , several colles castelleres ( teams that build towers ) attempt to build and dismantle a tower 's structure . on november 16 , 2010 , castells were declared by unesco to be amongst the masterpieces of the oral and intangible heritage of humanity . bbc , close-up : catalonia 's human towers
