image : matsumoto_castle_3.jpg thumb 350px the moat surrounding matsumoto castle a moat is a deep , broad ditch , either dry or filled with water , that is dug and surrounds a castle , fortification , building or town , historically to provide it with a preliminary line of defence . in some places moats evolved into more extensive water defences , including natural or artificial lakes , dams and sluices . in older fortifications , such as hillforts , they are usually referred to simply as ditches , although the function is similar . in later periods , moats or water defences may be largely ornamental . they could also act as a sewer .
