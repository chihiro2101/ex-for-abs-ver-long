daniel '' oz '' osbourne is a fictional character created by joss whedon for the television series buffy the vampire slayer . the character is portrayed by seth green . green also portrays the character in one episode of the spin-off series angel . in buffy , oz is a silence , guitar-playing teen who becomes willow ( alyson hannigan ) 's boyfriend . after first appearing in the episode '' inca mummy girl '' , oz discovers he is a werewolf in the episode '' phases '' and becomes a recurring character throughout the second season . green is elevated to main cast for the third season , and departs from the series in season four . green portrays the character for a final time in the season four finale '' restless '' as part of a dream sequence . the character made a brief canonical 'return ' , however , in the 2002 buffy the vampire slayer season eight storyline '' retreat '' .
