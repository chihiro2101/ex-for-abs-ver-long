aalst ; , brabantian : oilsjt ) is a city and municipality on the dender river , northwest from brussels in the flemish province of east flanders . the municipality comprises the city of aalst itself and the villages of baardegem , erembodegem , gijzegem , herdersem , hofstade , meldert , moorsel and nieuwerkerken . aalst is crossed by the molenbeek-ter erpenbeek in aalst and hofstade . the current mayor of aalst is christoph d'haese , from the new-flemish alliance party . the town has a long-standing ( folkloric ) feud with dendermonde ( north along the river ) , which dates from the middle ages .
