alternative tentacles is an independent record label established in 1979 in san francisco , california . it was used by dead kennedys for the self-produced single '' california über alles '' . after realizing the potential for an independent label , they released records for other bands as well . dead kennedys guitarist east bay ray and vocalist jello biafra formed alternative tentacles , but biafra became the owner in the mid-1980s . “ dead kennedys v . jello biafra ” alternative tentacles no longer owns the rights to dead kennedys recordings after a 2000 lawsuit .
