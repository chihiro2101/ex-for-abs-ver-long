cleckheaton is a town in the metropolitan borough of kirklees , in west yorkshire , england . historically part of the west riding of yorkshire , it is situated south of bradford , east of brighouse , west of batley and south-west of leeds . it is at the centre of the river spen and was the major town in the former borough of spenborough . cleckheaton has a history as a mill town , although this industry has now all but vanished .
