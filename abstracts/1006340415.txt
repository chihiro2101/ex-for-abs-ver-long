in 1952 , jrd founded an airline , known as air india ( later renamed tata airlines ) . in 1953 , the government of india passed the air corporations act and purchased a majority stake in the carrier from tata sons , though jrd tata would continue as chairman till 1977 . in 1945 , tata motors was founded , first focused on locomotives . in 1954 , it entered the commercial vehicle market after forming a joint venture with daimler-benz . in 1968 , tata consultancy services was founded .
