kåre isaachsen willoch ; born 3 october 1928 ) is a norway politician who served as prime minister of norway from 1981 to 1986 and as chairman of the høyre from 1970 to 1974 . he previously served as minister of trade and shipping in 1963 and 1965–1970 , and as president of the nordic council in 1973 . after stepping down as prime minister , he served as governor of oslo and akershus from 1989 to 1998 and as chairman of norway 's state broadcasting company nrk from 1998 to 2000 . since retiring from politics he has become an outspoken advocate of the environment and human rights , and has become widely respected for his activism also among the political left . he has written several books .
