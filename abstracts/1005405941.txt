tiswas ; an acronym of '' today is saturday : watch and smile '' ) is a british children 's television series that originally aired on saturday mornings from 5 january 1974 to 3 april 1982 , and was produced for the itv network by atv . it was created by atv continuity announcer peter tomlinson ( later to become a regular presenter on the show ) following a test period in 1973 when he tried out a few competitions and nonsense between the programmes .
