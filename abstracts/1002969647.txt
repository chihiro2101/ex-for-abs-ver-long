athina hélène onassis ( greek : αθηνά ωνάση ; born athina hélène roussel ( αθηνά ρουσσέλ ) , january 29 , 1985 associated press , '' first child of heiress : christina onassis gives birth to a girl '' , la times , january 29 , 1985 ) is a french-greek heiress and equestrian , the only surviving descendant of greek shipping magnate aristotle onassis , and the only child of aristotle 's daughter christina onassis .
