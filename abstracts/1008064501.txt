rothamsted research , previously known as the rothamsted experimental station and then the institute of arable crops research , is one of the oldest agricultural experiment station in the world , having been founded in 1843 . it is located at harpenden in the english county of hertfordshire and is a registered charity under english law . one of the station 's best known and longest-running experiments is the park grass experiment , a biological study that started in 1856 and has been continuously monitored ever since .
