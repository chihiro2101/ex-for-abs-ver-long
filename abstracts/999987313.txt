bioethics is the study of the ethics emerging from advances in biology and medicine . it is also moral discernment as it relates to medical policy and practice . bioethics are concerned with the ethical questions that arise in the relationships among life sciences , biotechnology , medicine and medical ethics , politics , law , theology and philosophy . it includes the study of values relating to primary care and other branches of medicine ( primary care ethics ) . ethics also relates to many other sciences outside the realm of biological sciences .
