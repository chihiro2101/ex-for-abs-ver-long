initiation is a rite of passage marking entrance or acceptance into a group or society . it could also be a formal admission to adulthood in a community or one of its formal components . in an extended sense it can also signify a transformation in which the initiate is 'reborn ' into a new role . examples of initiation ceremonies might include christian baptism or confirmation , jewish b'nai mitzvah , acceptance into a fraternal organization , secret society or religious order , or graduation from school or recruit training . a person taking the initiation ceremony in traditional rites , such as those depicted in these pictures , is called an initiate .
