dinas emrys is a rocky and wooded hillock near beddgelert in gwynedd , north-west wales . rising some above the floor of the glaslyn river valley , it overlooks the southern end of llyn dinas in snowdonia . little remains of the iron age hillfort or castle structures that once stood here , save its stone ramparts and the base of a keep . some believe the castle was erected by llewelyn the last to guard the road to the mountain pass of snowdon .
