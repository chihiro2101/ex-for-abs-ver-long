alexander yaroslavich nevsky ; ; 13 may 1221 & ndash ; 14 november 1263 ) , or alexander nevskii , served as prince of novgorod ( 1236–40 , 1241–56 and 1258-1259 ) , grand prince of kiev ( 1236–52 ) and grand prince of vladimir ( 1252–63 ) during some of the most difficult times in kievan rus ' history . commonly regarded as a key figure of kievan rus ' , alexander – a grandson of vsevolod the big nest – rose to legendary status on account of his military victories over german and swedish invaders . he preserved russian statehood and russian orthodoxy , agreeing to pay tribute to the powerful golden horde . metropolite macarius canonized alexander nevsky as a saint of the russian orthodox church in 1547 .
