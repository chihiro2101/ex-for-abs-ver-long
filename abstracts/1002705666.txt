jacques lipchitz 26 may 1973 ) was a cubism sculptor . lipchitz retained highly figurative and legible components in his work leading up to 1915–16 , after which naturalist and descriptive elements were muted , dominated by a synthetic style of crystal cubism . in 1920 lipchitz held his first solo exhibition , at léonce rosenberg 's galerie l'effort moderne in paris . fleeing the nazis he moved to the us and settled in new york city and eventually hastings-on-hudson .
