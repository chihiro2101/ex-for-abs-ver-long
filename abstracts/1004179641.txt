is a city and municipality in telemark in the county of vestfold og telemark in norway . it is part of the traditional region of grenland . the administrative centre of the municipality is the city of porsgrunn . the municipality of porsgrunn was established on 1 january 1838 ( see formannskapsdistrikt ) . the town of brevik and the rural district of eidanger were merged into the municipality of porsgrunn on 1 january 1964 . the conurbation of porsgrunn and skien is considered by statistics norway to be the seventh-largest city in norway .
