summertime ( released in the uk as summer madness ) is a 1955 british-american technicolor romance film directed by david lean and starring katharine hepburn , rossano brazzi , darren mcgavin , and isa miranda . variety film review ; june 8 , 1955 , page 6 . harrison 's reports film review ; june 11 , 1955 , pag 95 . the screenplay by lean and h.e . bates is based on the play the time of the cuckoo by arthur laurents .
