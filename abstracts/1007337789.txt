contact is a 1985 hard science fiction novel by american scientist carl sagan . it deals with the theme of contact between humanity and a more technologically advanced , extraterrestrial life form . it ranked no . 7 on the 1985 u.s . bestseller list . the novel originated as a screenplay by sagan and ann druyan ( whom he later married ) in 1979 ; when development of the film stalled , sagan decided to convert the stalled film into a novel . the film concept was subsequently revived and eventually released in 1997 as the film contact starring jodie foster .
