wilbur coleman sweatman ( february 7 , 1882 & ndash ; march 9 , 1961 ) was an american ragtime and dixieland jazz composer , bandleader and clarinetist . sweatman was one of the first african-american musicians to have fans nationwide . he was also a trailblazer in the racial integration of musical groups . berresford , mark . that 's got'em : the life and music of wilbur c . sweatman , university of mississippi press , 2010
