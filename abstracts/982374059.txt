thumb image : lage gouwespoorbrug.jpg thumb 200px light rail train at the swingbridge across the gouwe river the rijngouwelijn , or rgl , was a proposed light rail project in south holland , netherlands , that used some new tracks and some existing tracks from the gouda–alphen aan den rijn railway and the woerden–leiden railway . however , the new section might have used bus rapid transit instead . from 2003 to 2009 light rail vehicles operated on the gouda–alphen aan den rijn railway , sharing these tracks with regular ns stock . as of 2013 , only ns stock is used on the railway .
