mehmet fuat köprülü ( december 5 , 1890 & ndash ; june 28 , 1966 ) , also known as köprülüzade mehmed fuad , was a highly influential turkish sociology , turkology , scholar , minister of foreign affairs and deputy prime minister of the turkey . a descendant of the illustrious noble albanian köprülü family , whose influence in shaping ottoman history between 1656 and 1711 surpassed even that of the house of osman , fuat köprülü was a key figure in the intersection of scholarship and politics in early 20th century turkey .
