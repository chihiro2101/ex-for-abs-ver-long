herbert butros khaury tiny tim : tiptoe through a lifetime , lowell tarling , generation books , 2013 , p . 29 , the scribner encyclopaedia of american lives , vol . 4 , ed . kenneth t . jackson , 1998 , gale , p . 556 , ( april 12 , 1932 november 30 , 1996 ) , also known as herbert buckingham khaury and known professionally as tiny tim , was an american singer , ukulele player , and musical archivist . he is best remembered for his cover hits '' tiptoe through the tulips '' and '' livin ' in the sunlight , lovin ' in the moonlight '' , which he sang in a falsetto voice .
