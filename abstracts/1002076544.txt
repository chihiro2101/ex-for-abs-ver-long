lady bird lake ( formerly , and still colloquially referred to as town lake ) is a river-like reservoir on the colorado river in austin , texas , united states . the city of austin created the reservoir in 1960 as a cooling pond for a new city power plant . the lake , which has a surface area of , is now used primarily for recreation and flood control . the reservoir is named in honor of former first lady of the united states lady bird johnson . lady bird lake is the easternmost lake of a chain of reservoirs on the river , which is completely located in texas , and should not be confused with the larger colorado river located in the southwestern united states . this chain , known locally as the texas highland lakes , also includes lake buchanan , inks lake , lake lbj , lake marble falls , lake travis , and lake austin .
