trent 's last case is a detective novel written by e . c . bentley and first published in 1913 . its central character , the artist and amateur detective philip trent , reappeared subsequently in the novel trent 's own case ( 1936 ) , and the short-story collection trent intervenes ( 1938 ) . trent 's last case is actually the first novel philip trent appears in , and is a whodunit with a place in detective fiction history because it is the first major send-up of that genre . not only does trent fall in love with one of the primary suspects – usually considered off-limits – he also , after painstakingly collecting all the evidence , draws all the wrong conclusions . the novel was published as the woman in black in the united states , later in 1913 .
