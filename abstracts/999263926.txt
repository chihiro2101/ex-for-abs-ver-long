the airedale terrier ( often shortened to '' airedale '' ) , also called bingley terrier and waterside terrier , is a dog breed of the terrier type that originated in the valley ( dale ) of the river aire , in the west riding of yorkshire , england . it is traditionally called the '' king of terriers '' because it is the largest of the terrier breeds . the airedale was bred from the black and tan terrier ( now known as the welsh terrier ) , the otterhound and probably some other terrier breeds , and has contributed to other dog breeds , such as the yorkshire terrier . originally bred to serve as a versatile hunting and all around working farm dog , in britain this breed has also been used as a war dog , guide dog and police dog . in the united states , this breed has been used to hunt big game , upland birds , and water fowl , and serve in many other working capacities .
