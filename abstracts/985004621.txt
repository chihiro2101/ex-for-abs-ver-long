trypanosomatida is a group of kinetoplastid excavatas distinguished by having only a single flagellum . the name is derived from the greek trypano ( borer ) and soma ( body ) because of the corkscrew-like motion of some trypanosomatid species . all members are exclusively parasite , found primarily in insects . a few genera have life-cycles involving a secondary host , which may be a vertebrate , invertebrate or plant . these include several species that cause major diseases in humans .
