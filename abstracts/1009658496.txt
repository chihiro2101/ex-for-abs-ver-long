the lucayan people were the original residents of the bahamas before the european conquest of the americas . they were a branch of the tainos who inhabited most of the caribbean islands at the time . the lucayans were the first indigenous americans encountered by christopher columbus . shortly after contact , the spanish kidnapped and enslaved lucayans , with the genocide culminating in complete eradication of lucayan people from the bahamas by 1520 . the name '' lucayan '' is an anglicization of the spanish lucayos , itself a hispanicization derived in turn from the taino lukku-cairi ( which the people used for themselves ) , meaning '' people of the islands '' .
