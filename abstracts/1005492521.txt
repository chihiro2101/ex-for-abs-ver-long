pidgin ( formerly named gaim ) is a free and open-source multi-platform instant messaging client , based on a library named libpurple that has support for many instant messaging protocols , allowing the user to simultaneously login to various services from a single application , with a single interface for both popular and obsolete protocols ( from aol to discord ) , thus avoiding the hassle of having to deal with a new software for each device and protocol . the number of pidgin users was estimated to be over three million in 2007 .
