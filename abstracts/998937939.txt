plesetsk cosmodrome is a russian spaceport located in mirny , arkhangelsk oblast , about 800 & nbsp ; km north of moscow and approximately 200 & nbsp ; km south of arkhangelsk , the cosmodrome dates from 1957 . originally developed as an icbm site for the r-7 missile , it also served for numerous satellite launches using the r-7 and other rockets . its high latitude makes it useful only for certain types of launches , especially the molniya orbits , so for much of the site 's history it functioned as a secondary location , with most orbital launches taking place from baikonur , in the kazakh ssr . with the end of the soviet union , baikonur became a foreign territory , and kazakhstan charged 115 million usage fees annually . consequently , plesetsk has seen considerably more activity since the 2000s .
