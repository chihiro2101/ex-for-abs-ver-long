man of la mancha is a 1965 musical with a book by dale wasserman , music by mitch leigh , and lyrics by joe darion . it is adapted from wasserman 's non-musical 1959 teleplay i , don quixote , which was in turn inspired by miguel de cervantes and his 17th-century novel don quixote . it tells the story of the '' mad '' knight don quixote as a play within a play , performed by cervantes and his fellow prisoners as he awaits a hearing with the spanish inquisition .
