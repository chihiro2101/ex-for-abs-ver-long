norman milton lear ( born july 27 , 1922 ) is an american television writer and producer who created or developed many 1970s sitcoms such as all in the family , sanford and son , one day at a time and its 2017 remake , the jeffersons , good times , and maude . lear is a political activist , part of the so-called malibu mafia who funded liberal and progressive causes and politicians . lear was a silent partner of the nation magazine and founded the advocacy organization people for the american way in 1980 to counter the christian right in politics . he has supported first amendment rights .
