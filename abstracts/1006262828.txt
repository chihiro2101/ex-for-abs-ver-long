maurice edwin '' moon '' landrieu ( born july 23 , 1930 ) is an american politician from louisiana who served as the 56th mayor of new orleans from 1970 to 1978 . he also is a former judge . he represented new orleans ' twelfth ward in the louisiana house of representatives from 1960 to 1966 , served on the new orleans city council as a member at-large from 1966 to 1970 and was the united states secretary of housing and urban development under u.s . president jimmy carter from 1979 to 1981 . he is a member of the democratic party .
