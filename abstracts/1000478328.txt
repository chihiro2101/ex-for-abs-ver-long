joe versus the volcano is a 1990 american romantic comedy film written and directed by john patrick shanley and starring tom hanks and meg ryan . hanks plays a man who , after being told he is dying of a rare disease , accepts a financial offer to travel to a south pacific island and throw himself into a volcano on behalf of the superstitious natives . along the way , he meets and falls in love with the woman taking him there . the film received mixed reviews overall , but positive reviews from some critics , including roger ebert , who described the film as '' new and fresh and not shy of taking chances '' , and was a minor box office success in the us . it has since become a cult film .
