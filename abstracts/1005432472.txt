the 1993 long island rail road shooting occurred on december 7 , 1993 , aboard a long island rail road ( lirr ) train in garden city , new york in the united states . as the train arrived at merillon avenue station , passenger colin ferguson began firing at other passengers with a semi-automatic pistol . six of the victims were killed and nineteen others were wounded before ferguson was tackled and held down by other passengers on the train . ferguson 's trial was noted for a number of unusual developments , including his dismissing his defense counsel , insisting on representing himself , and questioning his victims on the stand . he was convicted in february 1995 , on six counts of murder and nineteen counts of attempted murder .
