the optic nerve , also known as cranial nerve ii , or simply as cn ii , is a paired cranial nerve that transmits visual information from the retina to the brain . in humans , the optic nerve is derived from optic stalks during the seventh week of development and is composed of retinal ganglion cell axons and glial cells ; it extends from the optic disc to the optic chiasma and continues as the optic tract to the lgn , pretectal area , and superior colliculus .
