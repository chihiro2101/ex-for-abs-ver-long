in november 2009 , the company discontinued its popular ciabatta sandwiches/burgers . in 2012 , jack in the box introduced a bacon milkshake as part of its '' marry bacon '' campaign . the sourdough jack , which uses two slices of sourdough bread with a hamburger patty , has been around since 1997 ( although it was first introduced in 1991 as the '' sourdough grilled burger '' ) . in october 2016 , the '' brunchfast '' items were introduced . those are bacon & egg chicken sandwich , blood orange fruit cooler , brunch burger , cranberry orange muffins , homestyle potatoes , and southwest scrambler plate . in january 2018 , the '' food truck series '' sandwiches were introduced , including the asian fried chicken , pork belly blt , and prime rib cheesesteak .
