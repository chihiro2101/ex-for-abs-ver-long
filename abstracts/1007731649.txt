is a 1962 black-and-white japanese jidaigeki film directed by akira kurosawa and starring toshiro mifune . it is a sequel to kurosawa 's 1961 yojimbo . originally an adaptation of the shūgorō yamamoto novel hibi heian , the script was altered with the success of kurosawa 's 1961 yojimbo to incorporate the lead character of that film .
