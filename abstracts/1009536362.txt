the wall street crash of 1929 , also known as the great crash , was a major american stock market crash that occurred in the fall of 1929 . it started in september and ended late in october , when share prices on the new york stock exchange collapsed . it was the most devastating stock market crash in the history of the united states , when taking into consideration the full extent and duration of its aftereffects . the great crash is associated with october 25 , 1929 , called black friday , the day after the largest sell-off of shares in u.s . history . • '' worst stock crash stemmed by banks ; 12,894,650 share day swaps market '' , the new york times , friday , october 25 , 1929 . retrieved november 27 , 2020 . & nbsp ; & nbsp ; • tom shachtman .
