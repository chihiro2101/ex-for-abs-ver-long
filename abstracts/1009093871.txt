in greek mythology , the rod of asclepius , , sometimes also spelled asklepios ) , also known as the staff of aesculapius and as the asklepian , is a serpent-entwined rod wielded by the greek god asclepius , a deity associated with healing and medicine . the symbol has continued to be used in modern times , where it is associated with medicine and health care , yet frequently confused with the staff of the god hermes , the caduceus . theories have been proposed about the greek origin of the symbol and its implications .
