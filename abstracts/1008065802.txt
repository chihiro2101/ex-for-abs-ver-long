spice ( '' simulation program with integrated circuit emphasis '' ) nagel , l . w , and pederson , d . o . , spice ( simulation program with integrated circuit emphasis ) , memorandum no . erl-m382 , university of california , berkeley , apr . 1973 nagel , laurence w . , spice2 : a computer program to simulate semiconductor circuits , memorandum no . erl-m520 , university of california , berkeley , may 1975 is a general-purpose , open-source analogue electronics simulator . it is a program used in integrated circuit and board-level design to check the integrity of circuit designs and to predict circuit behavior .
