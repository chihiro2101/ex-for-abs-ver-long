james fletcher hamilton henderson ( december 18 , 1897 & ndash ; december 29 , 1952 ) was an american pianist , bandleader , arranger and composer , important in the development of big band jazz and swing music . he was one of the most prolific black musical arrangers and , along with duke ellington , is considered one of the most influential arrangers and bandleaders in jazz history . henderson 's influence was vast . he helped bridge the gap between the dixieland and the swing eras . he was often known as '' smack '' henderson ( because of smacking sounds he made with his lips ) .
