gnaeus calpurnius piso ( latin : cn . calpurnius cn . f . cn . n . piso , c . 44 bc/43 bcad 20 ) , was a roman statesman during the reigns of augustus and tiberius . he served as consul in 7 bc , after which he was appointed governor of hispania and consul of africa . he belonged to one of rome 's most distinguished senatorial families , whose members included calpurnia , third wife of julius caesar .
