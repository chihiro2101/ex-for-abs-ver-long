die for the government is the debut album by the u.s . punk rock band anti-flag , released in 1996 . after this album , bassist andy flag played with anti-flag on their ep north america sucks , but left soon after as they could n't get along as a band . the cd booklet bids farewell to andy flag . the front cover gives the title '' die for the government '' , but side of the cd reads '' die for your government '' .
