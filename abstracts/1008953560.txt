manfred mann was an english rock band , formed in london and lasting from 1962 to 1969 . the group was named after their keyboardist manfred mann , who later led the successful 1970s group manfred mann 's earth band . the band had two different lead vocalists , paul jones from 1962 to 1966 , and mike d'abo from 1966 to 1969 . the group was regularly in the uk charts in the 1960s . three of the band 's most successful singles , '' do wah diddy diddy '' , '' pretty flamingo '' and '' mighty quinn '' , topped the uk singles chart . their 1964 hit '' 5-4-3-2-1 '' was the theme tune for the itv pop music show ready steady go ! . they were the first southern-england-based group to top the us billboard hot 100 during the british invasion .
