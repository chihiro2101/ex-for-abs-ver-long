preikestolen or prekestolen is a tourist attraction in the municipality of strand in rogaland county , norway . preikestolen is a steep cliff which rises above lysefjorden . atop the cliff , there is an almost flat top of approximately . preikestolen is located near the western part of the fjord , and on its north side . tourism at the site has been increasing in the early 21st century , with between 150,000 and 200,000 visitors in 2012 , making it one of the most visited natural tourist attractions in norway . base jumping often leap from the cliff . due to its increased popularity , the most used path to the site ( an long hike ) was improved by sherpas in 2013 .
