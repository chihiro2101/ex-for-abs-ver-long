medium frequency ( mf ) is the itu designation for radio frequency ( rf ) in the range of 300 & nbsp ; kilohertz ( khz ) to 3 & nbsp ; megahertz ( mhz ) . part of this band is the medium wave & nbsp ; ( mw ) am broadcast band . the mf band is also known as the hectometer band as the wavelengths range from ten to one hectometer ( 1000 to 100 & nbsp ; m ) . frequencies immediately below mf are denoted low frequency ( lf ) , while the first band of higher frequencies is known as high frequency ( hf ) . mf is mostly used for am radio , radio beacon , maritime ship-to-shore communication , and transoceanic air traffic control .
