new orleans rhythm and blues is a style of rhythm and blues music that originated in the u.s . city of new orleans . most popular from 1948 to 1955 , it was a direct precursor to rock and roll and strongly influenced ska . instrumentation typically includes drums , bass , piano , horns , electric guitar , and vocals . the style is characterized by syncopated '' second line '' rhythms , a strong backbeat , and soulful vocals . artists such as roy brown , dave bartholomew , and fats domino are representative of the new orleans r & b sound . mcknight mark , '' researching new orleans rhythm and blues , '' black music research journal 8/1 ( 1988 ) , 115 .
