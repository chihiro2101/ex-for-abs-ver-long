roeselare , , west flemish : roeseloare ) is a belgium city and municipality in the flemish province of west flanders . the municipality comprises the city of roeselare proper and the towns of beveren , oekene and rumbeke . the name of the city is derived from two germanic words meaning '' reed '' and '' open space '' , i.e . , a marsh in a forest glade . roeselare 's minor seminary is famous for having hosted the famous flemish poets guido gezelle , albrecht rodenbach and missionary jesuit constant lievens . the city is also home to the rodenbach brewery .
