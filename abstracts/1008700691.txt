james burke ( born 22 december 1936 ) is a british broadcaster , science historian , author , and television producer . he was one of the main presenters of the bbc1 science series tomorrow 's world from 1965 to 1971 and created and presented the popular television series connections ( 1978 ) , and its more philosophical sequel the day the universe changed ( 1985 ) , about the history of science and technology . the washington post has called him '' one of the most intriguing minds in the western world '' . connecting with one of the most intriguing minds in the western world , byline : ian starrett , 2005/06/03 , news letter ( belfast , northern ireland ) , the newspaper / bnet
