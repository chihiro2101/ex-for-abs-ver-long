brandenburg an der havel is a town in brandenburg , germany , which served as the capital of the margraviate of brandenburg until replaced by berlin in 1417 . with a population of 71,778 ( as of 2010 ) , it is located on the banks of the havel . the town of brandenburg provided the name for the medieval bishopric of brandenburg , the margraviate of brandenburg , and the current state of brandenburg . today it is a small town compared to nearby berlin , but it was the original nucleus of the former realms of brandenburg and prussia .
