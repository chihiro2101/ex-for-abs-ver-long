sophie 's choice is a 1979 novel by american author william styron . it concerns the relationships between three people sharing a boarding house in brooklyn : stingo , a young aspiring writer from the south , and the jewish scientist nathan landau and his lover sophie , a polish catholic survivor of the german nazi concentration camps , whom he befriends . sophie 's choice won the us national book award for fiction in 1980 . the novel was the basis of a 1982 film of the same name . it was controversial for the way in which it framed styron 's personal views regarding the holocaust .
