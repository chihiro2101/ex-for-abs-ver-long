thumb flexography ( often abbreviated to flexo ) is a form of printing process which utilizes a flexible relief plate . it is essentially a modern version of letterpress , evolved with high speed rotary functionality , which can be used for printing on almost any type of substrate , including plastic , metallic films , cellophane , and paper . it is widely used for printing on the non-porous substrates required for various types of food packaging ( it is also well suited for printing large areas of solid colour ) .
