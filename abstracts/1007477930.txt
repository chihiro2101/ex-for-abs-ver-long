a village green is a common open area within a village or other settlement . historically , a village green was common pasture with a pond for watering cattle and other stock , often at the edge of a rural settlement , used for gathering cattle to bring them later on to a common land for grazing . later , planned greens were built into the centres of villages . the village green also provided , and may still provide , an open-air meeting place for the local people , which may be used for public celebrations such as may day festivities . the term is used more broadly to encompass woodland , moorland , sports grounds , buildings , roads and urban parks .
