jean lesage , ; 10 june 1912 – 12 december 1980 ) was a canadian lawyer and politician from quebec . he served as the 19th premier of quebec from 22 june 1960 to 16 june 1966 . alongside georges-émile lapalme , rené lévesque and others , he is often viewed as the father of the quiet revolution . quebec city international airport was officially named in his honour on , and a provincial electoral district , jean-lesage , was named for him , as well .
