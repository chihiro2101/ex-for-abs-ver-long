meltham is a town and civil parish within the kirklees , in west yorkshire , england . it lies in the holme valley , below wessenden moor , four and a half miles south-west of huddersfield on the edge of the peak district . it had a population of 8,089 at the 2001 census , office for national statistics : census 2001 : parish headcounts : kirklees retrieved 3 september 2009 which was estimated to have increased to 8,600 by 2005 . the population assessed at the 2011 census was 8,534 .
