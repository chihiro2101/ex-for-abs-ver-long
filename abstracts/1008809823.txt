the parallax view is a 1974 american political thriller produced and directed by alan j . pakula , and starring warren beatty , hume cronyn , william daniels and paula prentiss . the screenplay by david giler and lorenzo semple jr . was based on the 1970 novel loren singer ( 1970 ) , the parallax view , by loren singer . robert towne did an robert towne . the story concerns a reporter 's investigation into a secretive organization , the parallax corporation , whose primary focus is political assassination . the parallax view is the second installment of pakula 's political paranoia trilogy , along with klute ( 1971 ) and all the president 's men ( 1976 ) . in addition to being the only film in the trilogy not to be distributed by warner bros . pictures , the parallax view is also the only one of the three not to be nominated for an academy award . it has received renewed praise in recent years .
