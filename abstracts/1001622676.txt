thumb steer wrestling , also known as bulldogging , is a rodeo event in which a horse-mounted rider chases a steer , drops from the horse to the steer , then wrestles the steer to the ground by grabbing its horns and pulling it off-balance so that it falls to the ground . the event carries a high risk of injury to the cowboy . there are some concerns from the animal rights community that the competition may include practices that constitute cruelty to animals , but the injury rate to animals is less than five-hundredths of one percent . a later prca survey of 60,971 animal performances at 198 rodeo performances and 73 sections of '' slack '' indicated 27 animals were injured , again approximately five-hundredths of 1 percent0.0004 .
