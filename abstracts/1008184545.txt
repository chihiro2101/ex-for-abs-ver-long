pao s . p . korolev rocket and space corporation energia , also known as rsc energia , rkk '' energiya '' ) , is a russian manufacturer of ballistic missile , spacecraft and space station components . the company is the prime developer and contractor of the russian crewed spaceflight program ; it also owns a majority of sea launch . its name is derived from sergei korolev , the first chief of its design bureau , and the russian word for energy .
