1917 in film was a particularly fruitful year for the art form , and is often cited as one of the years in the decade which contributed to the medium the most , along with 1913 . secondarily the year saw a limited global embrace of narrative film-making and featured innovative techniques such as continuity cutting . primarily , the year is an american landmark , as 1917 is the first year where the narrative and visual style is typified as '' classical hollywood '' . __toc__
