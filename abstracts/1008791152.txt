henan has some of the most advanced transportation system in china due to its flat terrain and its location at the heart of rise of central china . the jingguang and longhai railway , the nation 's two most important railways , run through much of the province and intersects at zhengzhou . other railway hubs such as shangqiu , xinxiang , and luohe have also become important centers of trade and manufacturing as a result . a high-speed railway links zhengzhou with xi'an . henan 's expressway system is highly developed and the total length is approximately , the highest total for any chinese province . the state of air transport is less stellar , the only 3 public airports are located in xinzheng ( near zhengzhou ) , luoyang , and nanyang .
