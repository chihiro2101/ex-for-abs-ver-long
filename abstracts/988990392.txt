braunston is a village and civil parish on the western edge of northamptonshire , england , next to the border with warwickshire . the parish had a population of 1,759 at the 2011 census . braunston is situated just off the a45 main road and lies between the towns of daventry and rugby . braunston is categorised by the office for national statistics as suburbs and small towns : suburbs there are 776 households in the village .
