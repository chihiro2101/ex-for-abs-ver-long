lúthien and beren are characters in the fantasy-world middle-earth , narrated by the english author j . r . r . tolkien . lúthien is an elf , daughter of thingol and melian . beren is a mortal man . the complex tale of their love for each other and the quest they are forced to follow , triumphing against overwhelming odds but ending in tragedy , appears in the silmarillion , the epic poem the lay of leithian , the grey annals section of the war of the jewels , and in other texts in tolkien 's legendarium , where it plays a central part . their story is told to frodo by aragorn in the lord of the rings . the story of beren and lúthien , immortal elf-maiden marrying a mortal man , and choosing mortality for herself , is mirrored in tolkien 's the tale of aragorn and arwen . their names appear on the grave of tolkien and his wife edith .
