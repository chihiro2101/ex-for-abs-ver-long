a speakeasy , also called a blind pig or blind tiger , is an illicit establishment that sells alcoholic beverages . such establishments came into prominence in the united states during the prohibition era ( 1920 & ndash ; 1933 , longer in some states ) . during that time , the sale , manufacture , and transportation ( rum-running ) of alcoholic beverages was illegal throughout the united states . '' speakeasy '' . merriam-webster . speakeasies largely disappeared after prohibition ended in 1933 , and the term is now often used to describe retro style bars .
