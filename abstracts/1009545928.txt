30 st mary axe ( known previously as the swiss re building ) , informally known as the gherkin , is a commercial skyscraper in london 's primary financial district , the city of london . it was completed in december 2003 and opened in april 2004 . with 41 floors , it is tall and stands on the former sites of the baltic exchange and chamber of shipping , which were extensively damaged in 1992 in the baltic exchange bombing by a device placed by the provisional ira in st mary axe , a narrow street leading north from leadenhall street . after plans to build the 92-story millennium tower were dropped , 30 st mary axe was designed by norman foster and arup group . it was erected by skanska ; construction started in 2001 . the building has become a recognisable landmark of london , and it is one of the city 's most widely recognised examples of contemporary architecture .
