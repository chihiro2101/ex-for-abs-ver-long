styrene is an organic compound with the chemical formula c 6 h 5 ch ch 2 . this derivative of benzene is a colorless oily liquid , although aged samples can appear yellowish . the compound evaporates easily and has a sweet smell , although high concentrations have a less pleasant odor . styrene is the precursor to polystyrene and several copolymers . approximately 25 & nbsp ; million tonnes of styrene were produced in 2010 , increasing to around 35 million tonnes by 2018 .
