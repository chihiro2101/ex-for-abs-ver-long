mrs . parkington is a 1944 drama film . variety film review ; september 20 , 1944 , page 10 . harrison 's reports film review ; september 23 , 1944 , page 156 . it tells the story of a woman 's life , told via flashbacks , from boarding house maid to society matron . the movie was adapted by polly james and robert thoeren from the novel by louis bromfield . it was directed by tay garnett and starred greer garson and walter pidgeon appearing together as husband and wife for the fourth time .
