absence of malice is a 1981 american drama neo noir thriller film directed by sydney pollack and starring paul newman , sally field , wilford brimley , melinda dillon and bob balaban . the title refers to one of the defenses against libel defamation , and is used in journalism classes to illustrate the conflict between disclosing damaging personal information and the public 's right to know . absence of malice ( 1981 ) when bad journalism kills , by lauren kirchner , columbia journalism review , july 15 , 2011
