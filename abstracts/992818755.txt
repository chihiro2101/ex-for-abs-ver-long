the nbc symphony orchestra was a radio orchestra conceived by david sarnoff , the president of the rca , especially for the celebrated conductor arturo toscanini . the nbc symphony performed weekly radio concert broadcasts with toscanini and other conductors and served as house orchestra for the nbc network . the orchestra 's first broadcast was on november 13 , 1937 and it continued until disbanded in 1954 . a new ensemble , independent of the network , called the symphony of the air , followed . it was made up of former members of the nbc symphony orchestra and performed from 1954 to 1963 , notably under leopold stokowski .
