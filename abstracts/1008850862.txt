piaggio & c . spa ( piaggio is an italy motor vehicle manufacturer , which produces a range of two-wheeled motor vehicles and compact commercial vehicles under seven brands : piaggio , vespa , gilera , aprilia , moto guzzi , derbi , and scarabeo . its corporate headquarters are located in pontedera , italy . the company was founded by rinaldo piaggio in 1884 , initially producing locomotives and railway carriages . piaggio 's subsidiaries employ a total of 7,053 employees and produced a total of 519,700 vehicles in 2014 . the manufacturer has six research-and-development centers and operates in over 50 countries .
