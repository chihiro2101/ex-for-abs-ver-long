dugald stewart ; 22 november 175311 june 1828 ) was a scottish philosopher and mathematician . today regarded as one of the most important figures of the later scottish enlightenment , he was renowned as a populariser of the work of francis hutcheson and adam smith . his lectures at the university of edinburgh were widely disseminated by his many influential students . in 1783 he was a joint founder of the royal society of edinburgh . in most contemporary documents he is referred to as prof dougal stewart . edinburgh post office directories 1800 to 1828 etc
