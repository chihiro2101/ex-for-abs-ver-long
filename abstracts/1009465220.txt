'' twain was also a staunch supporter of women 's rights and an active campaigner for women 's suffrage . his '' votes for women '' speech , in which he pressed for the granting of voting rights to women , is considered one of the most famous in history . helen keller benefited from twain 's support as she pursued her college education and publishing despite her disabilities and financial limitations . the two were friends for roughly 16 years . through twain 's efforts , the connecticut legislature voted a pension for prudence crandall , since 1995 connecticut 's official heroine , for her efforts towards the education of young african-american women in connecticut . twain also offered to purchase for her use her former house in canterbury , home of the canterbury female boarding school , but she declined .
