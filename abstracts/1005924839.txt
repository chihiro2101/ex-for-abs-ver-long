kshatriya ( from sanskrit kṣatra , '' rule , authority '' ) is one of the four varna ( social orders ) of hindu society , associated with warriorhood . the sanskrit term kṣatriyaḥ is used in the context of vedic society wherein members were organised into four classes : brahmin , kshatriya , vaishya and shudra . bujor avari ( 2007 ) . india : the ancient past : a history of the indian sub-continent from c . 7000 bc to ad 1200 , p . 89
