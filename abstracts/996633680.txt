a telegraph key is a specialized electrical switch used by a trained operator to transmit text messages in morse code in a telegraphy system . keys are used in all forms of electrical telegraph systems , such as landline or '' wire '' electrical telegraphy , and '' wireless '' , or radio telegraphy . an operator taps on the switch , connecting and disconnecting the electrical circuit , creating electrical pulses of two different lengths called '' dots '' and '' dashes '' , to spell out text messages in code .
