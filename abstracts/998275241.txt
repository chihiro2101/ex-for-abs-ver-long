thus spoke zarathustra : a book for all and none , also translated as thus spake zarathustra ) is a philosophical novel by german philosopher friedrich nietzsche , composed in four parts written and published between 1883 and 1885 . the first part was published in 1883 , the second and the third in 1884 and the last one in 1885 . much of the work deals with ideas such as the '' eternal return , '' the parable on the '' god is dead '' and the '' prophecy '' of the übermensch , which were first introduced in the gay science . charles guignon , and derk pereboom . 2001 . existentialism : basic writings . ( 2nd ed . ) . hackett . pp . 101–13 . nietzsche himself considered zarathustra to be his magnum opus .
