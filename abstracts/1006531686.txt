a wedding ring or wedding band is a finger ring that indicates that its wearer is married . it is usually forged from metal , and traditionally is forged of gold or another precious metal . western customs for wedding rings can be traced to ancient rome . depending on the culture , a wedding ring is typically worn on the base of the left or right ring finger ; if the wearer is left handed often it will go onto the right hand . many spouses wear their wedding rings day and night , causing an indentation in the skin that is visible even when the ring is removed .
