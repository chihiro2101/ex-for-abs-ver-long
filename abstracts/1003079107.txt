the culdees , & nbsp ; '' spouses of god '' ) were members of ascetic christian monastic and hermit communities of ireland , scotland , wales and england in the middle ages . appearing first in ireland and subsequently in scotland , attached to cathedral or collegiate churches , they lived in monastic fashion though not taking monastic vows . d'alton , edward .
