the battle of preveza was a naval battle that took place on 28 september 1538 near preveza in northwestern greece between an ottoman fleet and that of a holy league ( 1538 ) assembled by pope paul iii in which the ottoman navy defeated the allies . it occurred in the same area in the ionian sea as the battle of actium , 31 bc . it was one of the three largest sea battles that took place in the sixteenth century mediterranean , along with the battle of djerba and the battle of lepanto .
