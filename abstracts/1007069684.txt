schönbrunn palace ; central bavarian : schloss scheenbrunn ) was the main summer residence of the habsburg rulers , located in hietzing , vienna . the name schönbrunn ( meaning “ beautiful spring ” ) has its roots in an artesian well from which water was consumed by the court . the 1,441-room rococo palace is one of the most important architectural , cultural , and historic monuments in the country . the history of the palace and its vast gardens spans over 300 years , reflecting the changing tastes , interests , and aspirations of successive habsburg monarchs . it has been a major tourist attraction since the mid-1950s . official website
