michael rennie ( born eric alexander rennie ; 25 august 1909 & ndash ; 10 june 1971 ) was a british film , television and stage actor , perhaps best remembered for his starring role as the space visitor klaatu in the science fiction film the day the earth stood still ( 1951 ) . in a career spanning more than 30 years , rennie appeared in more than 50 films and in several american television series .
