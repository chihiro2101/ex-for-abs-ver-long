foucault 's pendulum ( original title : il pendolo di foucault is a novel by italy writer and philosopher umberto eco . it was first published in 1988 , and an english translation by william weaver appeared a year later . barber , malcolm . '' the trial of the templars '' second edition . cambridge university press , 2006 . page viii foucault 's pendulum is divided into ten segments represented by the ten sephirot . the satirical novel is full of esoteric references to kabbalah , alchemy , and conspiracy theory—so many that critic and novelist anthony burgess suggested that it needed an index . the pendulum of the title refers to foucault pendulum designed by french physicist léon foucault to demonstrate earth 's rotation , which has symbolic significance within the novel . some believe that it refers to michel foucault , the novel ends the day after michel foucault died ( 25th june 1984 ) . noting eco 's friendship with the french philosopher , d . defert , '' chronologie '' , in m . foucault , dits et écrits , gallimard , paris 1994 ( 2001 ) , p . 41 . but the author '' specifically rejects any intentional reference to michel foucault '' '' umberto eco & the open text '' by peter e . bondanella p . 133 —this is regarded as one of his subtle literary jokes .
