ilan ramon ; , born ilan wolferman ; june 20 , 1954 & x2013 ; february 1 , 2003 ) was an israel fighter pilot and later the first israeli astronaut . ramon was a space shuttle payload specialist of sts-107 , the fatal mission of columbia , in which he and six other crew members were killed in the re-entry accident . at 48 , he was the oldest member of the crew . ramon is the only foreign recipient of the united states congressional space medal of honor , which he was awarded posthumously .
