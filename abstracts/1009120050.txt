crowborough is a town and civil parish in the wealden district of east sussex , england . it is situated in the weald , at the edge of ashdown forest , in the high weald aonb . it is 7 & nbsp ; miles ( 11 & nbsp ; km ) south-west of royal tunbridge wells and 33 & nbsp ; miles ( 53 & nbsp ; km ) south of london . it has road and rail links and is served by a town council . it is the most populous inland town in east sussex , with over 20 fish .
