cyrix corporation was a microprocessor developer that was founded in 1988 in richardson , texas , as a specialist supplier of math coprocessors for 286 and 386 microprocessors . the company was founded by tom brightman and jerry rogers . cyrix founder , president , and ceo jerry rogers aggressively recruited engineers and pushed them , eventually assembling a design team of 30 people . cyrix merged with national semiconductor on november 11 , 1997 .
