the nuremberg rally ( officially , meaning reich party convention ) was the annual rally of the nazi party in germany , held from 1923 to 1938 . they were large nazi propaganda events , especially after adolf hitler 's rise to power in 1933 . these events were held at the nazi party rally grounds in nuremberg from 1933 to 1938 and are usually referred to in english as the '' nuremberg rallies '' . many films were made to commemorate them , including leni riefenstahl 's triumph of the will and the victory of faith .
