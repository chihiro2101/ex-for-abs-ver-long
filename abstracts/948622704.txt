broad street is a major thoroughfare and popular nightclub centre in central birmingham , england . traditionally , broad street was considered to be outside birmingham city centre , but as the city centre expanded with the removal of the inner ring road , broad street has been incorporated into the new westside district of the city centre due to its position within the a4540 road . broad street is also the centre of birmingham 's banking and financial centre . it can boast region head offices of lloyds banking group , royal bank of scotland , deutsche bank , hsbc and many other banking organisations . more than 15,000 people are employed in this sector , in this area of birmingham .
