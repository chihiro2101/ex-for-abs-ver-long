michael ian grade , baron grade of yarmouth , ( born 8 march 1943 ) is an english media proprietor and businessman . he has held a number of senior roles in television , including controller of bbc1 ( 1984–1986 ) , chief executive of channel 4 ( 1988–1997 ) , chairman of the bbc ( 2004–2006 ) , and executive chairman of itv plc ( 2007–2009 ) . since 2011 , he has been a conservative party life peer in the house of lords .
