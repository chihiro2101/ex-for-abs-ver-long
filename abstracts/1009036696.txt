the greatest generation , also known as the g.i . generation and the world war ii generation , is the demography cohort following the lost generation and preceding the silent generation . the generation is generally defined as people born from 1901 to 1927 . they were shaped by the great depression and were the primary participants in world war ii . following the war , this generation birthed the baby boomers and later became the grandparents of generation x and the millennials .
