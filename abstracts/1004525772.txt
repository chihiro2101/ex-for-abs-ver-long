oatmeal is a type of coarse flour made of hulled oat grains ( groats ) that have either been mill ( grinding ) or steel-cut . ground oats are also called '' white oats '' . steel-cut oats are known as '' coarse oatmeal '' , '' irish oatmeal '' or '' pinhead oats '' . rolled oats can be either thick or thin , and may be categorized as '' old-fashioned '' , '' quick '' , or '' instant '' , depending on the cooking time , which is shortened by the size of the oats , precooking , and sometimes the addition of enzymes . the term 'oatmeal ' is also used in the u.s . and parts of canada to describe a popular oat porridge made from either ground , steel-cut , or rolled oats .
