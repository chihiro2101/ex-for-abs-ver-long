max simon nordau ( born simon maximilian südfeld ; july 29 , 1849 – january 23 , 1923 ) was a zionism leader , physician , author , and social critic . social science and the politics of modern jewish identity , mitchell bryan hart he was a co-founder of the zionist organization together with theodor herzl , and president or vice president of several zionist congresses . as a social critic , he wrote the conventional lies of our civilisation ( 1883 ) , degeneration ( 1892 ) , and paradoxes ( 1896 ) . although not his most popular or successful work whilst alive , degeneration is the book most often remembered and cited today .
