veolia transport ( formerly connex and cgea transport ) was the international transport services division of the french-based multinational company veolia environnement until the 2011 merger that gave rise to transdev . veolia transport traded under the brand names of veolia transportation in north america and israel , veolia transport , veolia verkehr in germany and with the former name connex preserved in lebanon , melbourne ( until it ceased operations in 2009 ) and mybus ( until it ceased operations on 31 december 2012 ) . until 2011 , veolia had diverse road and rail operations across the globe , employing 72,000 workers worldwide and serving completely or partly about 40 metropolitan areas with more than 1,000,000 inhabitants .
