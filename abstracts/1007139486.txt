prince ferdinand philippe of orléans ( 3 september 1810 – 13 july 1842 ) was the eldest son of louis philippe d'orléans , duke of orléans ( the future louis philippe i ) and maria amalia of naples and sicily . born in exile in his mother 's native sicily , he was heir to the house of orléans from birth . following his father 's succession as king of france in 1830 , he became the crown prince and subsequently duke of orléans ( french : duc d'orléans ) , the title by which he is best known . he died in 1842 , never to succeed his father or see the collapse of the july monarchy and subsequent exile of his family to england .
