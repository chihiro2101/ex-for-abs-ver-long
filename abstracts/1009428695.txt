murder on the orient express is a work of detective fiction by england writer agatha christie featuring the belgian detective hercule poirot . it was first published in the united kingdom by the collins crime club on 1 january 1934 . in the united states , it was published on 28 february 1934 , under the title of murder in the calais coach , by dodd , mead and company . the uk edition retailed at seven shillings and sixpence ( 7/6 ) and the us edition at 2 . the elegant train of the 1930s , the orient express , is stopped by heavy snowfall . a murder is discovered , and poirot 's trip home to london from the middle east is interrupted to solve the murder . the us title of murder in the calais coach was used to avoid confusion with the 1932 graham greene novel stamboul train , which had been published in the united states as orient express .
