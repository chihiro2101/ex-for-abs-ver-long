the thunderbirds are assigned to the 57th wing , and are based at nellis air force base , nevada . created in 1953 , the usaf thunderbirds are the third-oldest formal flying aerobatic team ( under the same name ) in the world , after the french air force patrouille de france formed in 1931 and the united states navy blue angels formed in 1946 . the thunderbirds squadron tours the united states and much of the world , performing aerobatic formation and solo flying in specially marked aircraft . the squadron 's name is taken from the legendary creature that appears in the mythologies of several indigenous north american cultures .
