the royal concertgebouw orchestra , is a symphony orchestra in the netherlands , based at the amsterdam concertgebouw ( concert hall ) . considered one of the world 's leading orchestras , in a widely cited list compiled by the british magazine gramophone in 2008 ( the world ’ s greatest orchestras ) , the rco was in the number one position . a new list published in 2015 , by the website bachtrack , ( mark pullinger , chailly and the berliner philharmoniker : the critics ’ choice for world ’ s best conductor and orchestra , 3 september 2015 ) , also based on a poll among music critics , put the concertgebouw orchestra in the number two position . queen beatrix conferred the '' royal '' title upon the orchestra in 1988 .
