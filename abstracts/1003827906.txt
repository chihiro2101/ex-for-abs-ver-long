ellen taaffe zwilich ; born april 30 , 1939 ) is an american composer , the first female composer to win the pulitzer prize for music . her early works are marked by atonal exploration , but by the late 1980s , she had shifted to a postmodernist , neoromantic style . she has been called '' one of america 's most frequently played and genuinely popular living composers .
