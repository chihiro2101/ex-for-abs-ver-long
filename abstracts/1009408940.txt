raï , , sometimes written rai , is a form of algerian folk music that dates back to the 1920s . singers of raï are called cheb ( arabic : شاب ) ( or shabab , i.e . young ) as opposed to sheikh ( arabic : شيخ ) ( shaykh , i.e . old ) , the name given to chaabi singers . the tradition arose in the city of oran , primarily among the poor . traditionally sung by men , by the end of the 20th century , female singers had become common . the lyrics of raï have concerned social issues such as disease and the policing of european colonies that affected native populations . gross , joan , david mcmurray , and ted swedenburg .
