ath ; , , is a belgium municipality located in the walloon province of hainaut . the ath municipality includes the old communes of lanquesaint , irchonwelz , ormeignies , bouvignies , ostiches , rebaix , maffle , arbre , houtaing , ligne , mainvault , moulbaix , villers-notre-dame , villers-saint-amand , ghislenghien , isières , meslin-l'evêque , and gibecq . ath is known as the '' city of giants '' after the ducasse d'ath festivities which take place every year on the fourth weekend in august . huge figures representing goliath , samson , and other allegoric figures are paraded through the streets , and goliath 's wedding and his famous fight with david are re-enacted .
