alternative 3 is a television programme , broadcast once only in the united kingdom in 1977 , and later broadcast in australia , canada , and new zealand , as a fictional hoax , an heir to orson welles ' the war of the worlds ( radio ) . purporting to be an investigation into the uk 's contemporary '' brain drain '' , alternative 3 uncovered a plan to make the moon and mars habitable in the event of climate change and a terminal environmental catastrophe on earth . the programme was originally meant to be broadcast on april fools ' day , 1977 . while its broadcast was delayed until 20 june , the credits explicitly date the film to 1 april . alternative 3 ended with credits for the actors involved in the production and featured interviews with a fictitious american astronaut .
