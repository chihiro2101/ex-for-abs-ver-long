antoine pierre joseph marie barnave ( 22 october 176129 november 1793 ) was a french politician , and , together with honoré mirabeau , one of the most influential orators of the early part of the french revolution . he is most notable for correspondence with marie antoinette in an attempt to set up a constitutional monarchy and for being one of the founding members of the feuillants . françois furet and mona ozouf , eds . a critical dictionary of the french revolution ( 1989 ) , pp 186–96
