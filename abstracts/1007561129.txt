stalag 17 is a 1953 comedy-drama war film which tells the story of a group of american airmen held in a german world war ii prisoner of war camp , who come to suspect that one of their number is an informant . the film was adapted by billy wilder and edwin blum from the broadway play of the same name by donald bevan and edmund trzcinski , which was based on their experiences as prisoners in stalag 17b in austria . produced and directed by wilder , it starred william holden in his oscar-winning role , don taylor , robert strauss , harvey lembeck , peter graves , neville brand , richard erdman , sig ruman , and otto preminger . strauss and lembeck both appeared in the original broadway production .
