return to the forbidden planet is a jukebox musical by playwright bob carlton based on shakespeare 's the tempest and the 1956 science fiction film forbidden planet ( which itself drew its plot loosely from the tempest ) . it was billed as '' shakespeare 's forgotten rock and roll masterpiece '' . return to the forbidden planet started life with the bubble theatre company as a production for open-air performance in a tent . a revised version of the musical opened , indoors , at the everyman theatre in liverpool in the mid-1980s . it later moved to the tricycle theatre in london . after some rework a final version opened the cambridge theatre in london 's west end in september 1989 . it won the olivier award for best new musical for both 1989 and 1990 .
