a piano trio is a group of piano and two other instruments , usually a violin and a cello , or a piece of music written for such a group . it is one of the most common forms found in classical chamber music . the term can also refer to a group of musicians who regularly play this repertoire together ; for a number of well-known piano trios , see below . the term '' piano trio '' is also used in jazz , where it most commonly designates a pianist accompanied by bass and drums . other jazz trios exist , such as piano , bass and guitar .
