janet miriam holland taylor caldwell ( september 7 , 1900august 30 , 1985 ) was a british-born american novelist and prolific author of popular fiction , also known by the pen names marcus holland and max reiner , and by her married name of j . miriam reback . in her fiction , she often used real historical events or persons . taylor caldwell 's best-known works include dynasty of death , dear and glorious physician ( about saint luke ) , ceremony of the innocent , pillar of iron ( about cicero ) , the earth is the lord 's ( about genghis khan ) and captains and the kings . her last major novel , answer as a man , appeared in 1980 .
