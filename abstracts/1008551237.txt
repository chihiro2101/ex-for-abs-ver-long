the rhodesian ridgeback is a dog breed bred in the southern africa region . its forebears can be traced to the ridged hunting dogs of the khoekhoe , which were crossed with european dogs by the early colonists of the cape colony of southern africa . the original breed standard was drafted by f . r . barnes , in bulawayo , southern rhodesia ( now zimbabwe ) , in 1922 , who named the breed the rhodesian ridgeback . the standard was approved by the south african kennel union in 1927 .
