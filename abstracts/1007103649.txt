altun ha is the name given to the ruins of an ancient mayan city in belize , located in the belize district about north of belize city and about west of the shore of the caribbean sea . the site covers an area of about . stones from the ruins of the ancient structures were reused for residential construction of the agricultural village of rockstone pond in modern times , but the ancient site did not come to the attention of archeologists until 1963 . the old northern highway connects altun ha to belize 's northern highway , and the site is accessible for tourism . the largest of altun ha 's temple-pyramids , the '' temple of the masonry altars '' , is high . a drawing of this structure is the logo of belize 's leading brand of beer , '' belikin '' .
