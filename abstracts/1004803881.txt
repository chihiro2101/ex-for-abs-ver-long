peter brown ( born july 11 , 1953 ) is an american singer-songwriter and record producer . brown was a popular performer in the late 1970s and early 1980s . his biggest success was the release of the lp in 1977 called a fantasy love affair which produced the disco hits '' do ya wan na get funky with me '' and '' dance with me '' . he wrote , with robert rans , madonna 's hit '' material girl '' .
