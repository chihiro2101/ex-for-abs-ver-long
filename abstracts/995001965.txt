the daughters of bilitis , also called the dob or the daughters , was the first lesbian civil and political rights organization in the united states . the organization , formed in san francisco in 1955 , was conceived as a social alternative to lesbian bars , which were subject to raids and police harassment . as the dob gained members , their focus shifted to providing support to women who were afraid to come out . the dob educated them about their rights , and about gay history . the historian lillian faderman declared , '' its very establishment in the midst of witch-hunts and police harassment was an act of courage , since members always had to fear that they were under attack , not because of what they did , but merely because of who they were . '' faderman , p . 190-191 the daughters of bilitis endured for 14 years , becoming an educational resource for lesbians , gay men , researchers and mental health professionals .
