adolf ( c . 1255 – 2 july 1298 ) was house of nassau from about 1276 and elected king of the romans ( king of germany ) from 1292 until his deposition by the prince-electors in 1298 . he was never crowned by the pope , which would have secured him the title of holy roman emperor . he was the first physically and mentally healthy ruler of the holy roman empire ever to be deposed without a papal excommunication . adolf died shortly afterwards in the battle of göllheim fighting against his successor albert of habsburg . he was the second in the succession of so-called count-kings of several rivalling comital houses striving after the roman-german royal dignity . his last agnatic dynastic descendant was william iv of luxembourg .
