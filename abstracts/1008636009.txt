sauerkraut ; , lit . '' sour cabbage '' ) is finely cut raw cabbage that has been fermented by various lactic acid bacteria . it has a long shelf life and a distinctive sour flavour , both of which result from the lactic acid formed when the bacteria ferment the sugars in the cabbage leaves . gil marks . encyclopedia of jewish food . p . 1052 . joseph mercola , brian vaszily , kendra pearsall , nancy lee bentley . dr . mercola 's total health cookbook & program . p . 227 .
