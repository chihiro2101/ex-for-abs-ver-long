the plains of abraham is a historic area within the battlefields park in quebec city , quebec , canada . the land is the site of the battle of the plains of abraham , which took place on 13 september 1759 , but hundreds of acres of the fields became used for grazing , housing , and minor industrial structures . only in 1908 was the land ceded to quebec city , though administered by the specifically created and federally-run national battlefields commission . the park is today used by 4 million visitors and tourists annually for sports , relaxation , outdoor concerts , and festivals .
