an axle or axletree is a central shaft for a rotation wheel or gear . on wheeled vehicles , the axle may be fixed to the wheels , rotating with them , or fixed to the vehicle , with the wheels rotating around the axle . in the former case , bearings or bushings are provided at the mounting points where the axle is supported . in the latter case , a bearing or bushing sits inside a central hole in the wheel to allow the wheel or gear to rotate around the axle . sometimes , especially on bicycles , the latter type axle is referred to as a spindle .
