tony russell '' charles '' brown ( september 13 , 1922 & ndash ; january 21 , 1999 ) was an american blues singer and pianist whose soft-toned , slow-paced blues-club style influenced blues performance in the 1940s and 1950s . between 1949 and 1952 , brown had seven top 10 hits in the u.s . billboard r & b chart . his best-selling recordings included '' driftin ' blues '' and '' merry christmas baby '' .
