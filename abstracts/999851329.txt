wieliczka ( german : groß salze , latin : magnum sal ) is a historic town in southern poland , situated within the kraków metropolitan area in lesser poland voivodeship since 1999 . the town was initially founded in 1290 by przemysł ii . nowadays , it is mostly known for the wieliczka salt mine , declared a unesco world heritage site in 1978 , and the historic old town core which was listed as one of national polish monuments in 1994 . the population in 2019 was estimated at 23,395 .
