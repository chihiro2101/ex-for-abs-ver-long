juho kusti paasikivi ; 27 november 1870 – 14 december 1956 ) was the seventh president of finland ( 1946–1956 ) . representing the finnish party and the national coalition party , he also served as prime minister of finland ( 1918 and 1944–1946 ) , and was an influential figure in finnish economics and politics for over fifty years . he is remembered as a main architect of finland 's foreign policy after the second world war . .
