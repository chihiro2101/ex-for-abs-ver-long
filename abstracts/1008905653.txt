abdullah el-faisal ( born trevor william forrest , also known as abdullah al-faisal , sheikh faisal , sheik faisal , and imam al-jamaikee , born 10 september 1963 ) is a muslim cleric who preached in the united kingdom until he was convicted of stirring up racial hatred and urging his followers to murder jews , hindus , christians , and americans . el-faisal was sentenced to nine years in prison , of which he served four years before being deported to jamaica in 2007 . he subsequently traveled to africa , but was deported from botswana in 2009 and from kenya back to jamaica in january 2010 .
