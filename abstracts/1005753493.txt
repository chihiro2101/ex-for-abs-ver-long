the jerky boys : the movie , also known as the jerky boys , is a 1995 american crime/comedy film starring john g . brennan and kamal ahmed , best known as the comedy duo the jerky boys . the film features two men , johnny b . and kamal , aka '' the jerky boys '' , two self-described '' low-lifes from queens '' , who get into trouble with the mafia when one of their prank calls leads them into a money laundering business . the jerky boys discover that their long-loved hometown bar will be torn down unless they stop their shenanigans .
