albert barnes ( december 1 , 1798 – december 24 , 1870 ) was an american theology , born in rome , new york . he graduated from hamilton college , clinton , new york , in 1820 , and from princeton theological seminary in 1823 . barnes was ordained as a presbyterian minister by the presbytery of elizabethtown , new jersey , in 1825 , and was the pastor successively of the presbyterian church in morristown , new jersey ( 1825–1830 ) , and of the first presbyterian church ( philadelphia ) ( 1830–1868 ) . barnes is best known for his extensive bible commentary and notes on the old and new testaments , published in a total of 14 volumes in the 1830s .
