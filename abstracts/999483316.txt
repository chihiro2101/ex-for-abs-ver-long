tourism in sweden comprised a relatively small part of the swedish economy in 2011 at 2.9 % of the country 's gdp ; at this time , tourism generated 264 billion swedish krona , 98.8 billion of which was foreign-visitor expenditure in sweden . 7.1 % of swedish household income is spent on domestic tourism . one well-known tourist route is via train from southern to northern sweden , viewing historical , natural and cultural attractions . this route is particularly popular among german tourists . according to the cia world factbook , sweden was the 21st most-visited country in the world , with 7,627,000 arrivals in 2006 .
