aurora ( 2016 population 55,445 ) is a town in central york region in the greater toronto area , within the golden horseshoe of southern ontario , canada . it is located north of the city of richmond hill and is partially situated on the oak ridges moraine . in the canada 2016 census , the municipal population of aurora was the 95th largest in canada , compared to 97th for the 2006 census . aurora has been ranked in the top 10 wealthiest towns in canada .
