the bad seed is a 1954 novel by american writer william march , the last of his major works published before his death . nominated for the 1955 national book award , the bad seed tells the story of a mother 's realization that her young daughter is a murderer . its enormous critical and commercial success was largely realized after march 's death only one month after publication . in 1954 , the novel was adapted into a successful and long-running broadway play by maxwell anderson , and in 1956 into an academy award-nominated film directed by mervyn leroy .
