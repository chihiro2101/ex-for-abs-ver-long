demonology is the study of demons or beliefs about demons , and the hierarchy of demons . they may be nonhuman , separable souls , or discarnate spirits which have never inhabited a body . a sharp distinction is often drawn between these two classes , notably by the melanesians , several african groups , and others . the islamic jinn , for example , are not reducible to modified human souls . at the same time these classes are frequently conceived as producing identical results , e.g . diseases .
