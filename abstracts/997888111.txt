calmodulin ( cam ) ( an abbreviation for calcium-modulated protein ) is a multifunctional intermediate calcium-binding messenger protein expressed in all eukaryote . it is an intracellular target of the secondary messenger ca 2+ , and the binding of ca 2+ is required for the activation of calmodulin . once bound to ca 2+ , calmodulin acts as part of a calcium signal transduction by modifying its interactions with various target proteins such as kinases or phosphatases .
