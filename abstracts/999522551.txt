a cassette deck is a type of tape machine for playing and recording audio cassettes that does not have built-in power amplifier or speakers or both , and serves primarily as a transport . it can be a part of a automotive entertainment system , or a part of a portable mini system , or a part of a home component system . in the latter case it is also called a component cassette deck or just a component deck . a '' tape recorder '' is a more generic term to identify a device that usually has a self-contained power amplifier and either has a built-in speaker or comes packaged with ones .
