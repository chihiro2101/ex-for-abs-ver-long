eliyahu ben-shaul cohen & lrm ; , & lrm ; ; 6 december 1924 & nbsp ; – 18 may 1965 ) , commonly known as eli cohen , was an israeli spy . he is best known for his espionage work in 1961–65 in syria , where he developed close relationships with the syrian political and military hierarchy , and became the chief adviser to the minister of defense . syrian counterintelligence eventually uncovered the spy conspiracy and convicted cohen under pre-war martial law , sentencing him to death and hanging him publicly in 1965 .
