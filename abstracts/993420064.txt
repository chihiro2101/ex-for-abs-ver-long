aphasiology is the study of language impairment usually resulting from brain damage , due to neurovascular accident—hemorrhage , stroke—or associated with a variety of neurodegenerative diseases , including different types of dementia . it is also the name of a scientific journal covering the area . these specific language deficits , termed aphasias , may be defined as impairments of language production or comprehension that can not be attributed to trivial causes such as deafness or oral paralysis . a number of aphasias have been described , but two are best known : expressive aphasia ( broca 's aphasia ) and receptive aphasia ( wernicke 's or sensory aphasia ) .
