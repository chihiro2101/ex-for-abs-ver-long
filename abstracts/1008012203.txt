the ruling class is a 1972 british black comedy film . variety film review ; 19 april 1972 , page 18 . it is an adaptation of peter barnes ' satire stage play the ruling class which tells the story of a paranoid schizophrenia british nobleman ( played by peter o'toole ) who inherits a peerage . the film co-stars alastair sim , william mervyn , coral browne , harry andrews , carolyn seymour , james villiers and arthur lowe . it was produced by jules buck and directed by peter medak . the film has been described as a '' commercial failure & nbsp ; . that has since become a cult classic '' ; peter o'toole described it as '' a comedy with tragic relief '' .
