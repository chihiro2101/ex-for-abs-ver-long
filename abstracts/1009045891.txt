thomas john barnardo ( 4 july 184519 september 1905 ) was an irish-born british philanthropist and founder and director of homes for poor and deprived children . from the foundation of the first barnardo 's home in 1867 to the date of barnardo 's death , nearly 60,000 children had been taken in . although barnardo never finished his studies at the london hospital , he used the title of 'doctor ' and later secured a licentiate .
