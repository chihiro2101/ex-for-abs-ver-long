the palearctic or palaearctic is the largest of the eight biogeographic realms of the earth . it stretches across all of eurasia north of the foothills of the himalayas , and north africa . the realm consists of several ecoregions : the euro-siberian region ; the mediterranean basin ; the sahara and arabian deserts ; and western , central and east asia . the palaearctic realm also has numerous rivers and lakes , forming several freshwater ecoregions . the term 'palearctic ' was first used in the 19th century , and is still in use as the basis for zoogeography classification .
