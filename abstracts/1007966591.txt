uss george h.w . bush ( cvn-77 ) is the tenth and final supercarrier of the united states navy . she is named for the 41st president of the united states and former director of central intelligence george h . w . bush , who was a naval aviator during world war ii . the vessel 's callsign is avenger , after the tbm avenger aircraft flown by then-lieutenant george h.w . bush in world war ii . construction began in 2003 at the northrop grumman newport news shipyard 's drydock 12 , the largest in the western hemisphere . she was completed in 2009 at a cost of 6.2 & nbsp ; billion and her home port is naval station norfolk , virginia .
