the yellow brick road is a fictional element in the 1900 children 's novel the wonderful wizard of oz by american author l . frank baum . the road also appears in the several sequel oz books such as the marvelous land of oz ( 1904 ) and the patchwork girl of oz ( 1913 ) . the road 's most notable portrayal is in the classic 1939 mgm musical film the wizard of oz , loosely based on baum 's first oz book . in the novel 's first edition the road is mostly referred to as the '' road of yellow bricks '' . in the original story and in later films based on it such as the wiz ( 1978 ) , dorothy gale must find the road before embarking on her journey , as the tornado did not deposit her farmhouse directly in front of it as in the 1939 film .
