robert bourassa , ; july 14 , 1933 & ndash ; october 2 , 1996 ) was a canadian politician from quebec . he served as the 22nd premier of quebec in two different mandates , first from may 12 , 1970 , to november 25 , 1976 , and then from december 12 , 1985 , to january 11 , 1994 , serving a total of just under 15 years as provincial premier .
