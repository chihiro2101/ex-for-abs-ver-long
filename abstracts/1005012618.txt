barchester towers is a novel by english author anthony trollope published by longmans in 1857 . it is the second book in the chronicles of barsetshire series , preceded by the warden and followed by doctor thorne . among other things it satirises the antipathy in the church of england between high church and evangelical adherents . trollope began writing this book in 1855 . he wrote constantly and made himself a writing-desk so he could continue writing while travelling by train . '' pray know that when a man begins writing a book he never gives over '' , he wrote in a letter during this period .
