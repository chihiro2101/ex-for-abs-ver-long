aphrodite 's child was a greek progressive rock band formed in 1967 , by vangelis ( keyboards , flutes ) , demis roussos ( bass , acoustic and electric guitar , vocals ) , loukas sideras ( drums and vocals ) , and silver koulouris ( guitar ) . the band 's career ended shortly after release of their most influential album , 666 , which has appeared on a number of lists of the top progressive or psychedelic albums of all time .
