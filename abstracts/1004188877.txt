prof jacobus cornelius kapteyn frs frse lld ( 19 january 1851 – 18 june 1922 ) was a dutch astronomy . he carried out extensive studies of the milky way and was the discoverer of evidence for galactic rotation . kapteyn was also among the first to suggest the existence of dark matter using stellar velocities as early as 1922 . kapteyn , jacobus cornelius ( 1922 ) .
