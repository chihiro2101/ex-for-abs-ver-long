by mid-1942 , the results of the battles of the coral sea and midway had averted the perceived threat of invasion . despite the '' beat germany first '' policy , 346,000 americans were fighting in the pacific in 1942 . curtin had previously opposed conscription for overseas service during world war i , and again in 1940 when it was introduced by menzies , although he had supported softening it to allow conscripts to serve in australian territories . curtin recognised that the restriction of the australian conscripts to australia and its territories was morally indefensible and politically unviable . to remove what was now a major obstacle to the government 's re-election prospects , he moved to remove the restriction . while there was support from the communist party of australia and its sympathisers , there was trenchant opposition from the catholic church , represented by b . a . santamaria and arthur calwell . ultimately , the defence act was amended so that conscripts could be deployed outside of australia to '' such other territories in the south-west pacific area as the governor-general proclaims as being territories associated with the defence of australia '' . the stress of the war and especially this bitter internal battle within labor took a great toll on curtin 's health , which had never been robust even at the best of times . he had suffered all of his life from stress-related illnesses , and depression ; he was also a heavy smoker .
