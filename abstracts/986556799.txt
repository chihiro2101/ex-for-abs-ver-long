the bobby fuller four ( sometimes stylized as bobby fuller 4 ) was a popular mid-1960s american rock & roll band started by bobby fuller . with its first incarnation formed in 1962 in el paso , texas , the group went on to produce some of its most memorable hits under mustang records in hollywood , california . the band 's most successful songs include '' let her dance '' , '' i fought the law '' , and '' love 's made a fool of you .
