zwijndrecht is both a village and a municipality located in the flemish province of antwerp , in belgium . as well as zwijndrecht proper , the municipality includes the village of burcht . as of january 1 , 2006 , zwijndrecht had a total population of 18,231 . the total area is 17.82 & nbsp ; km² which gives a population density of 1,023 inhabitants per km² .
