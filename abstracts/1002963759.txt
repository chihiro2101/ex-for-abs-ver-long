wild river is a 1960 american drama film directed by elia kazan and starring montgomery clift , lee remick , jo van fleet , albert salmi and jay c . flippen . the film was shot on location in the tennessee valley , and was adapted by paul osborn from two novels : borden deal 's dunbar 's cove ( 1957 ) and william bradford huie 's mud on the stars ( 1942 ) , drawing for plot from deal 's story of a battle of wills between the nascent tennessee valley authority and generations-old land owners , and from huie 's study of a rural southern matriarchal family for characters and their reaction to destruction of their land , as well as the then-controversial employment of african-american laborers by the tva . it marked bruce dern 's film debut . in 2002 , the film was selected for the united states national film registry by the library of congress as being '' culturally , historically , or aesthetically significant '' .
