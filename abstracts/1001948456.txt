the battle of shrewsbury was a battle fought on 21 july 1403 , waged between an army led by the lancastrian king henry iv and a rebel army led by henry percy ( hotspur ) from northumberland . the battle , the first in which english archers fought each other on english soil , reaffirmed the effectiveness of the longbow and ended the percy challenge to king henry iv of england . part of the fighting is believed to have taken place at what is now battlefield , shropshire , england , three miles north of the centre of shrewsbury . it is marked today by battlefield church and battlefield heritage park .
