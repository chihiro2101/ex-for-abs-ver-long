this sporting life is a 1963 british kitchen sink drama film directed by lindsay anderson . based on the this sporting life ( novel ) by david storey , which won the 1960 macmillan fiction award , it recounts the story of a rugby league footballer , frank machin , in wakefield , a mining town in yorkshire , whose romantic life is not as successful as his sporting life . storey , a former professional rugby league footballer , also wrote the screenplay . the film stars richard harris , rachel roberts , william hartnell and alan badel . the film was harris 's first starring role , and won him the best actor award at the 1963 cannes film festival . he was also nominated for an oscar for academy award for best actor . roberts won her second bafta award and received an academy award for best actress . harris was nominated for the bafta that year as well . the film opened at the odeon leicester square in london 's west end on 7 february 1963 . the times , 7 february 1963 , page 2
