charles pinckney ( october 26 , 1757october 29 , 1824 ) was an american planter and politician who was a signer of the united states constitution . he was elected and served as the 37th governor of south carolina , later serving two more non-consecutive terms . he also served as a us senator and a member of the house of representatives . he was first cousin once removed of fellow signer charles cotesworth pinckney . pinckney 's descendants included seven future south carolina governors , including men related to the maybank and rhett families .
