manos hatzidakis ( also spelled hadjidakis ; ; 23 october 1925 – 15 june 1994 ) was a greek composer and theorist of greek music , widely considered as one of the greatest greek songwriters of all time . his legacy and contribution is widespread among the works of contemporary greek music , through the second half of the 20th and into the 21st century . he was also one of the main proponents of the '' éntekhno '' form of music ( along with mikis theodorakis ) . in 1960 , he received an academy award for best original song for his song never on sunday from the never on sunday .
