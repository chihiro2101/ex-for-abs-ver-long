the bonnot gang ( la bande à bonnot ) was a french criminal anarchism group that operated in france and belgium during the belle époque , from 1911 to 1912 . composed of individuals who identified with the emerging illegalism milieu , the gang used cutting-edge technology ( including automobiles and repeating rifles ) not yet available to the french police . originally referred to by the press as simply '' the auto bandits '' , the gang was dubbed '' the bonnot gang '' after jules bonnot gave an interview at the office of le petit parisien , a popular daily paper . bonnot 's perceived prominence within the group was later reinforced by his high-profile death during a shootout with french police in choisy-le-roi .
