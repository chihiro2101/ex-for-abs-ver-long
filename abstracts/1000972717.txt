virginia satir ( 26 june 1916 – 10 september 1988 ) was an influential american author and psychotherapist , recognized for her approach to family therapy . her pioneering work in the field of family reconstruction therapy honored her with the title . '' mother of family therapy '' her most well-known books are conjoint family therapy , 1964 , peoplemaking , 1972 , and the new peoplemaking , 1988 . she is also known for creating the virginia satir change process model , a psychological model developed through clinical studies . change management and organizational gurus of the 1990s and 2000s embrace this model to define how change impacts organizations . international human learning resources network the satir model : yesterday and today , contemporary family therapy volume 24 , number 1 , 7–22
