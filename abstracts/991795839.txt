collusion is a deceitful agreement or secret cooperation between two or more parties to limit open competition by deceiving , misleading or defrauding others of their legal right . collusion is not always considered illegal . it can be used to attain objectives forbidden by law ; for example , by defrauding or gaining an unfair market advantage . it is an agreement among firms or individuals to divide a market , set prices , limit production or limit opportunities . it can involve '' unions , wage fixing , kickbacks , or misrepresenting the independence of the relationship between the colluding parties '' . collusion law & legal definition in legal terms , all acts effected by collusion are considered void . collusion . archived 2009-10-31 .
