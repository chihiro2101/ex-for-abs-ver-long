seymour is a census-designated place ( cdp ) and unincorporated community in blount and sevier counties in the u.s . state of tennessee . the cdp population was 10,919 at the 2010 u.s . census . u.s . census bureau , quickfacts - seymour , tennessee . retrieved : february 7 , 2012 . it is included in the knoxville , tennessee knoxville metropolitan area .
