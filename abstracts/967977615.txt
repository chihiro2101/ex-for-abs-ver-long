robert fitzhamon ( died march 1107 ) , or robert fitzhamon ( literally , 'robert , son of hamon ' ) , seigneur de creully in the calvados region and torigny in the manche region of normandy , was the first norman feudal baron of gloucester and the norman conqueror of glamorgan , southern wales . he became lord of glamorgan in 1075 . as a kinsman of the conqueror and one of the few anglo-norman barons to remain loyal to the two successive kings william rufus and henry i of england , he was a prominent figure in england and normandy .
