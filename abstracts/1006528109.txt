daniel paul johns ( born 22 april 1979 ) is an australian musician , singer , and songwriter best known as the former front man and lyricist of the rock band silverchair . johns is also one half of the dissociatives with paul mac , one half of dreams with luke steele , and released his first solo album , talk , in 2015 . in 2007 , johns was ranked at number 18 on rolling stones list of the 25 most underrated guitarists .
