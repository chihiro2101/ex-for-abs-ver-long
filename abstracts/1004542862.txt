capricorn one is a 1978 thriller film in which a reporter discovers that a supposed mars landing by a crewed mission to the planet has been faked via a conspiracy involving the government and - under duress - the crew themselves . it was written and directed by peter hyams and produced by lew grade 's itc entertainment . it stars elliott gould with james brolin , sam waterston , and o.j . simpson as the astronauts . the music score was created by jerry goldsmith .
