image : caillebotteautoportrait.jpg thumb portrait de l'artiste ( self-portrait ) . c . 1892 . musée d'orsay , paris.gustave caillebotte ; 19 august 1848 – 21 february 1894 ) was a french painter who was a member and patron of the impressionists , although he painted in a more realistic manner than many others in the group . caillebotte was noted for his early interest in photography as an art form .
