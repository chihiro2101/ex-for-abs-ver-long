thumb image registration is the process of transforming different sets of data into one coordinate system . data may be multiple photographs , data from different sensors , times , depths , or viewpoints . lisa gottesfeld brown , a survey of image registration techniques ( abstract ) , acm computing surveys archive , volume 24 , issue 4 , december 1992 ) , pages 325 - 376 it is used in computer vision , medical imaging , biological imaging and brain mapping military automatic target recognition , and compiling and analyzing images and data from satellites . registration is necessary in order to be able to compare or integrate the data obtained from these different measurements .
