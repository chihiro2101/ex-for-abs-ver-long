'' pictorial to sleep '' , time , march 8 , 1937 . the magazine 's success stimulated many imitators , such as look , which was founded a year later in 1937 and ran until 1971 . luce moved life into its own building at 19 west 31st street , a beaux-arts architecture jewel built in 1894 . it is considered a building of '' outstanding significance '' by the new york landmarks preservation commission . later life moved its editorial offices to 9 rockefeller plaza .
