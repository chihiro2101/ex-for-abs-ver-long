mount parnassus ; , parnassos ) is a mountain of limestone in central greece that towers above delphi , north of the gulf of corinth , and offers scenic views of the surrounding olive groves and countryside . according to greek mythology , this mountain was sacred to dionysus and the dionysian mysteries ; it was also sacred to apollo and the corycian nymphs , and it was the home of the muses . the mountain was also favored by the dorians . it is suggested that the name derives from parnassas , the possessive adjective of the luwian word parna meaning house , or specifically temple , so the name effectively means the mountain of the house of the god . palmer , leonard r . , 1961 , '' mycenaeans and minoans '' , pp.241-2
