dendermonde , french : termonde ) is a city in the flemish province of east flanders in belgium . the municipality comprises the city of dendermonde and the towns of appels , baasrode , grembergen , mespelare , oudegem , schoonaarde , and sint-gillis-bij-dendermonde . dendermonde is at the mouth of the river dender , where it flows into the scheldt . the town has a long-standing folkloric feud with aalst , south along the same river , which dates from the middle ages . the city is an administrative , commercial , educational , and medical centre for the surrounding region . the current mayor of dendermonde is piet buyse ( christen-democratisch en vlaams ) .
