ramiro i ( c . 790 & nbsp ; – 1 february 850 ) was king of asturias ( modern-day spain ) from 842 until his death in 850 . son of king bermudo i , he became king following a succession struggle after his predecessor , alfonso ii , died without children . during his turbulent reign , he fended off attacks from both vikings and moors . architecturally , his recreational palace santa maría del naranco and other buildings used the ramirense style that prefigured romanesque architecture . he was a contemporary of abd ar-rahman ii , umayyad emir of córdoba .
