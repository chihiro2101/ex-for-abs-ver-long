theodore von kármán ; 11 may 18816 may 1963 ) was a hungarian-american mathematician , aerospace engineer , and physicist who was active primarily in the fields of aeronautics and astronautics . he was responsible for many key advances in aerodynamics , notably on supersonic and hypersonic airflow characterization . he is regarded as the outstanding aerodynamic theoretician of the 20th century . iris chang , thread of the silkworm , basic books , 1996 , pages 47–60
