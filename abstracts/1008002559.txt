schadenfreude ; ; 'harm-joy ' ) is the experience of pleasure , joy , or self-satisfaction that comes from learning of or witnessing the troubles , failures , or humiliation of another . schadenfreude is a complex emotion where , rather than feeling sympathy , one takes pleasure from watching someone 's misfortune . this emotion is displayed more in children than adults . however , adults also experience schadenfreude , although generally they conceal it .
