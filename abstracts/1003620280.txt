elbow room : the varieties of free will worth wanting is a 1984 book by the american philosopher daniel dennett , in which dennett discusses the philosophy issues of free will and determinism . in 1983 , dennett delivered the john locke lectures at oxford on the topic of free will . in 1984 , these ideas were published in the book elbow room : the varieties of free will worth wanting . in this book daniel dennett explored what it means for people to have free will . the title , elbow room , is a reference to the question : '' are we deterministic machines with no real freedom of action or do we in fact have some elbow room , some real choice in our behavior ? '' .
