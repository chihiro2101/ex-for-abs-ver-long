goodnight moon is an american children 's book written by margaret wise brown and illustrated by clement hurd . it was published on september 3 , 1947 , and is a highly acclaimed bedtime story . this book is the second in brown and hurd 's '' classic series '' , which also includes the runaway bunny and my world . the three books have been published together as a collection titled over the moon . brown , margaret wise and clement hurd . over the moon : a collection of first books ( harpercollins , 2006 ) .
