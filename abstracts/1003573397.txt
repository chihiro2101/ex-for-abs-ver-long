the royal college of music is a music school established by royal charter in 1882 , located in south kensington , london , uk . it offers training from the undergraduate to the doctoral level in all aspects of western music including performance , composition , conducting , music theory and history . the rcm also undertakes research , with particular strengths in performance practice and performance science . the college is one of the four conservatories of the associated board of the royal schools of music and a member of conservatoires uk . its buildings are directly opposite the royal albert hall on prince consort road , next to imperial college and among the museums and cultural centres of albertopolis .
