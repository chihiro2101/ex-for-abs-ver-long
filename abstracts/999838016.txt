cheddar gorge is a limestone gorge in the mendip hills , near the village of cheddar , somerset , england . the gorge is the site of the cheddar show caves , where britain 's oldest complete human skeleton , cheddar man , estimated to be 9,000 years old , was found in 1903 . older remains from the upper paleolithic era ( 12,000â€“13,000 years ago ) have been found . the caves , produced by the activity of an underground river , contain stalactites and stalagmites . the gorge is part of a site of special scientific interest called cheddar complex . cheddar gorge , including the caves and other attractions , has become a tourist destination . in a 2005 poll of radio times readers , following its appearance on the television programme seven natural wonders ( 2005 ) , cheddar gorge was named as the second greatest natural wonder in britain , surpassed only by dan yr ogof caves . the gorge attracts about 500,000 visitors per year .
