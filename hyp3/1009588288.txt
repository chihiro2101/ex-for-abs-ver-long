savigny was born at frankfurt , of a family recorded in the history of lorraine , deriving its name from the castle of savigny near charmes in the valley of the moselle in 1803 savigny published das recht des besitzes ( the law of possession ) archive.org anton thibaut hailed it as a masterpiece which brought the old uncritical study of roman law to an end in 1808 savigny was appointed full professor of roman law at landshut in 1810 he was appointed to the chair of roman law at the new university of berlin , chiefly at the insistence of wilhelm von humboldt he was engaged in lecturing , in government of the university ( of which he was the third rector ) , and as tutor to the crown prince in roman , criminal and prussian law during his time in berlin , savigny befriended barthold georg niebuhr and karl friedrich eichhorn savigny argued that such a codification of the law would have an adverse effect savigny opposed this conception of legal science to the '' historical study of positive law '' , which according to him is '' a condition precedent to right understanding of the science of all law '' ) savigny made known to the world the discovery by niebuhr in verona of the lost institutes of gaius savigny intended it to be a literary history of roman law from irnerius to the present time in 1835 savigny began his elaborate work on contemporary roman law , ( 8 vols major publications by savigny include : english translation by thomas erskine perry ( 1848 ) : '' von savigny 's treatise on possession : or , the jus possessionis of the civil law '' , ( 6 ed english translation of volume 1 by holloway , william ( 1867 ) : '' system of the modern roman law , volume i ''
