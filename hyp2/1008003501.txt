league butt was instrumental in fostering links between constitutional and revolutionary nationalism through his representation of members of the fenians society in court it was designed to mobilise public opinion behind the demand for an irish parliament , with , as he put it , '' full control over our domestic affairs '' he believed that home rule would promote friendship between ireland and
