savigny was born at frankfurt , of a family recorded in the history of lorraine , deriving its name from the castle of savigny near charmes in the valley of the moselle after the fashion of german students , savigny visited several universities , notably jena , leipzig and halle ; and returning to marburg , took his doctorate in 1800 at marburg he lectured as privatdozent on criminal law and the pandects in 1803 savigny published das recht des besitzes ( the law of possession ) in 1808 savigny was appointed full professor of roman law at landshut in 1810 he was appointed to the chair of roman law at the new university of berlin , chiefly at the insistence of wilhelm von humboldt during his time in berlin , savigny befriended barthold georg niebuhr and karl friedrich eichhorn in 1814 savigny wrote the pamphlet ( of the vocation of our age for legislation and legal science ) savigny argued that such a codification of the law would have an adverse effect savigny opposed this conception of legal science to the '' historical study of positive law '' , which according to him is '' a condition precedent to right understanding of the science of all law '' savigny intended it to be a literary history of roman law from irnerius to the present time in the first volume , savigny treated the history of roman law from the breaking up of the empire until the beginning of the 12th century it was a supplement to his work on modern roman law , in which he again argued for the necessity of the historical treatment of law his son , karl friedrich von savigny ( 1814â€“1875 ) , was prussian minister of foreign affairs in 1849
