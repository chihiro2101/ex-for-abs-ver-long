subsequently played coriolanus in the spread of the eagle ( bbc , 1963 ) and sir toby belch for the bbc television shakespeare production of twelfth night in 1980 . over the years , hardy played a range of parts on television and film . his first continuing role in a tv series was as businessman alec stewart in the award-winning oil company drama the troubleshooters for the bbc , which he played from 1966 to 1970 . he won further acclaim for his portrayal of the mentally-unhinged abwehr sgt . gratz in lwt 's 1969 war drama manhunt . in 1975 , hardy portrayed albert , prince consort in the award-winning 13-hour serial edward the seventh ( known as edward the king to the american audience ) , which he regarded as one of his best performances .
